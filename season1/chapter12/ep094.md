The ship sails on the blue sea now. The water is warm and bright and calm. The sun is high above the waves. The deck is warm under her feet. Daru sits up on the deck. He cannot stay still today. He looks out at the wide water. His small head turns this way and that. The waves slap softly against the ship.

Then Daru jumps up in his red Pod. "I know this sea!" he cries out. "I know it! This is my sea!" His voice rings high across the deck. The blue water is the same as home. The same warm sea by the white rocks. Daru rolls fast along the rail. He looks and looks at the bright waves. "Croatia! We go to Croatia!" His old home is near now. Ako-chan smiles to see him so glad.

But then Daru grows quiet for a bit. He stops by the rail and looks back. The sea runs east, far behind them. Somewhere out there is the red desert. Somewhere out there is Gabu. Gabu walks his red rocks alone now. Daru is the only turtle here. Dango comes and sits beside him. He leans his soft side on the Pod. Obia stands at the rail, quiet and still. Daru does not talk for a moment.

Then Daru lifts his head up again. "Gabu is home," he says softly. "I go home too." His voice is glad but a little small. Ako-chan puts her hand on his Pod. The warm wind smells of salt and home. The sea carries them on to the west. The white rocks of home are near now.
