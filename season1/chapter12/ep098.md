The sea is grey and cold this morning. A thin mist hangs low on the water. The ship moves slowly through the Channel. The cold air bites at her face. Ako-chan stands close to the glass. She looks hard into the grey ahead. Something pale grows in the mist.

Then the mist pulls back a little. White cliffs rise up from the sea. Tall and bright above the grey water. They shine softly in the morning light. More and more white grows in the mist. The cliffs stand long along the shore. Ako-chan's heart beats fast and warm. She knows these cliffs very well. This is the white coast of home. Obia comes and stands beside her. It looks out at the tall white cliffs. "England," it says. "We are here." Its voice is quiet but sure.

Dango jumps up at the window. He presses his nose to the cold glass. His breath makes a small white cloud. His tail wags fast behind him. His whole body shakes with joy. He whines and looks up at Ako-chan. He scratches softly at the cold glass. He knows this place too, deep inside. Home. The word is in his eyes. Ako-chan laughs and holds him close.

The white cliffs grow tall and near. The gulls cry out above the ship. Ako-chan keeps her eyes on the land. So long away, and now so near. Her eyes are wet and bright at once. The mist lifts off the bright water. The white cliffs fill the whole sky now. England waits beyond the white cliffs. The long, long road is almost done. And the ship sails slowly home.
