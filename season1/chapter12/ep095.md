The ship comes to the white rocks at last. The sea is clear and blue and bright. This is the coast of Croatia. This is Daru's home. The evening sun is low and warm. The Pod stops by the warm stones. Daru looks out at the rocks and sea.

Daru jumps out of his red Pod. He runs fast over the white rocks. "Home! I am home!" he calls. His little legs move quickly on the stone. He runs in a big circle on the rocks. Then, all at once, he is gone. He runs behind a rock and stops. The shore is quiet now. Ako-chan waits and watches hard. Where is Daru? Is this goodbye? Then a small round shape pops up. "Goodbye!" Daru calls out loud. He waves a little leg in the air.

Dango runs to the rail and barks. He wags his long fluffy tail fast. Obia stands quiet and still as ever. Ako-chan laughs, but her eyes are wet. "Goodbye, Daru!" she calls back to him. The warm wind moves over the white rocks. The same dry grass smell is here. It is just like the first day, long ago. Ako-chan thinks of the shy small turtle. He grew so bold on the long road.

Then Daru is gone again, behind the rock. This time he does not come back. Ako-chan keeps her eyes on the stones. The white rocks are warm and still. Daru is home, fast and glad to the end. Now they are three. Ako-chan, Dango, and Obia. The old rocks shine in the evening sun. The sea is gold and still.
