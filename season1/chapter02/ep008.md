It is morning in London. The station is very big. It is called St Pancras. The roof is high and made of glass. Light falls down on the floor. Many people walk fast. They carry big bags. Long trains wait by the platforms. Ako-chan looks up. The roof is far above her. Dango stays close at her feet.

"This way," says Obia. It walks to a long blue train. The train is the Eurostar. It is very long. It goes all the way to France. Ako-chan stops by the door. Her heart beats fast. This is the real start of the journey.

They step on board. But Dango stops at the door. He does not jump up yet. First he smells the floor. Then he smells the step. He looks up and down the train. He checks each new thing with care. He always does this. He sniffs the seat. He sniffs under the window. He looks all around too. Then he is happy. He climbs up beside Ako-chan.

Obia sits across from them. It is quiet and still. Ako-chan looks out the window. The platform is gray and busy. A bell sounds soft and clear. A door closes far away. Soft voices speak all around them. The train is warm inside. Dango puts his head on her leg.

Then the train moves. It is slow at first. The platform slides away. London slides away too. The train goes faster and faster. Ako-chan holds the edge of the window. Her face is bright. She cannot sit still. The green fields open up ahead. A new country waits far away. Their journey starts now, to the south.
