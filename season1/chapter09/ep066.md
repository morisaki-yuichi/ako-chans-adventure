The day stays bright and cold. Pen-san walks beside Ako-chan now. He is so happy to be near her. But his little legs get tired. The ice is cold under his feet. He looks up at Ako-chan. He lifts his small grey wings. "Pick me up," he says. "Please."

Ako-chan smiles down at him. She bends down low. She picks Pen-san up in her arms. He is soft and round and warm. His grey down is like a little cloud. He cheeps and presses close. "So warm!" he says. Ako-chan holds him to her chest. She never held a thing so soft.

Then Dango sees this. His ears go flat. His tail drops down low. He pushes in between her legs. He looks up at the penguin in her arms. He does not like it. Not at all. This is the worst one yet. First came Daru. Then Gabu. Then Suke-san. And now a soft, round penguin in her arms. Ako-chan looks down at him. "Oh, Dango," she says. She puts one hand on his head. But her other arm still holds Pen-san. Dango makes a long, low sound. He turns his face away from her.

Daru rolls his Pod closer to see. He looks up at Pen-san in her arms. "He walks!" says Daru. "He walks. Like us!" Gabu watches too, quiet and still. Suke-san stands at her right side. Pen-san looks down at Dango. He does not understand. He just wants to be near Ako-chan too. Ako-chan kneels down on the ice. Now she can hold them both. One arm for Pen-san. One hand for Dango. "I am here for you too," she says. The white world is cold. But her arms are full and warm.
