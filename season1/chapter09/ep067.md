The next day is grey and still. Ako-chan stands on the ice. Pen-san is close at her feet. Then she sees a new shape. It comes from far off. It is bigger than Pen-san. It walks in a straight line. Step, step, step. It does not stop. It does not turn.

The new bird comes right up to them. It is a tall penguin. Its back is black. Its front is white. There is a white ring around each eye. Its beak is short and round. It stands very straight. It looks at everyone, one by one. Then its eyes stop on Obia. "I am Colonel Adelie," he says. His voice is hard and sharp. "I watch over Pen-san." Then he looks back at Ako-chan. "Tell me about Obia," he says.

Pen-san hops to the big penguin. "This is Ako-chan!" he says. "She came!" But Colonel Adelie does not soften. He stands tall and stiff. His eyes stay on Obia. He is small, but he is not afraid. Suke-san steps up at Ako-chan's right. He looks at the Colonel. Two careful animals look at each other. Neither one looks away. Dango stands close too. The cold wind moves over the ice. No one speaks for a moment.

Ako-chan looks at Obia. Then at the Colonel. "Obia is my friend," she says. "Obia came all this way with us. Obia helps us." The Colonel does not answer that. He just watches Obia. His eyes are small and hard. Obia stands still and quiet. It does not move away. But the Colonel keeps watching it. Something new is on the ice now.
