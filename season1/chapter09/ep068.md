The day is calm on the ice. The wind is soft and low today. The sun is bright on the white land. It makes the ice shine like glass. Ako-chan sits on a box from the ship. Her coat keeps her warm. Pen-san sits close beside her.

Daru and Gabu come out in their Pods. The Pods roll over the hard ice. They are warm inside, even here. The red light and the blue light glow. Pen-san watches them with big eyes. "What are those?" he asks. "They are Pods," says Daru. "We walk, and they go!" Daru runs inside his Pod. It rolls fast over the ice. Pen-san cheeps and hurries after it. He slips and falls, then gets up again. Gabu rolls along slowly and steadily. Pen-san waddles beside the blue Pod now. "You are slow," he says. "I like slow." Gabu does not say much. But he stays right by Pen-san. Pen-san likes his new friends.

Dango lies on the ice in his warm vest. His boots keep his feet off the cold. Suke-san sits at Ako-chan's right, on watch. Obia stands near the ship, quiet. The red collar sits at its neck, as always. The cold wind moves the snow in thin lines. It runs low over the ice like white smoke. But everyone stays warm. Obia made good things for the cold. The sun is bright, and the ice throws the light back. It shines under their feet and all around.

Pen-san climbs into Ako-chan's lap. He is tired from his play. He closes his big black eyes. Daru and Gabu rest in their warm Pods. Dango leans on her other side. For a while, no one needs to move. The ice shines all around them. The world is calm and white.
