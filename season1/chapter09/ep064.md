They stand together on the white land. The sky is pale and wide. Ako-chan takes her first steps out. Her friends move along with her. The ice creaks under their boots. It is a long, low sound. Their breath turns white in the cold air.

They walk on the ice near the ship. The white land is too cold and too wide. There is no home out here. Only the ship keeps them warm and safe. So they stay close to it. They do not go far. They cannot. Then, far away, she sees small black shapes. They stand on the ice together. There are many of them. "Look," she says. "What are those? Those dark things, way out there?" The shapes do not move much. They are too far to hear. Ako-chan watches them for a long time.

Suke-san walks at her right side. His nose works hard in the wind. He smells the new land — the ice, the cold, and something far off. He does not like all of it. "Strange," he says. "I do not know this place." His ears turn left and right. Dango walks slowly at her other side. He keeps low to the ice. Daru and Gabu stay in their warm Pods.

Ako-chan stops and looks around her. The white ice runs all the way to the sky. The silence is deep and full. She hears only the wind and the creaking ice. Her breath makes small white clouds. She pulls her coat tight around her. The cold finds every little gap. Even so, she wants to see more. The far black shapes are still out there. She does not know what they are yet. But the white world is not empty. Something lives out here too.
