The ship moves slowly now. It comes close to the white land. Ahead, the open sea ends. The ice begins. A great white shelf rests on the water. Obia turns the wheel with care. The side of the ship touches the ice. It stops there. "We are here," says Obia. "This is the white land."

Ako-chan looks out at the ice. It is wide and flat. It shines white. Obia puts a board down to the ice. Then it looks at Ako-chan. "You go first," it says. Ako-chan holds her breath. She steps onto the board. Then she steps down onto the ice. Her boot presses on it. The ice is hard under her feet. It does not break. She stands on the white land. "Oh," she says. Her voice is low. She is really here.

One by one, the others come down. Dango steps onto the ice. He sniffs it and pulls his nose back. It is so cold. Then Suke-san comes. He stops on the white ice. He closes his eyes. He takes a long, deep breath. He smells the air from every side. North and south, east and west. He reads the whole white land. His ears stand up. "New," he says. "It is all new."

Ako-chan looks all around her. There is white everywhere. White ice, white sky, white land. The ship looks small behind them now. No tree. No rock. No green. Just ice under her feet. The world is cold and bright and still. They came so far for this day. Now the white land is real. And they stand on top of it.
