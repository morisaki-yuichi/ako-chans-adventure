The next day, they walk out on the ice. They go a little farther than before. But the ship still shows behind them. Ako-chan can see it, small and dark. The white land is huge and flat. The cold air is sharp and clean.

Then they come to the far ice. Tall hills of ice stand all around. They are white on top. But deep inside, they are blue. The sun shines through the ice. It makes long blue shadows on the ground. Ako-chan stops and looks up. "They are so beautiful," she says. Pen-san waddles to her side. "I know them!" he says. "This is my home." The ice shapes rise high above them. The wind cut them into curves and points. Some stand tall like towers. Some are low and round. The ice is old and very hard.

Colonel Adelie comes with them. He walks behind Obia. Step, step, step, in a straight line. His eyes do not leave the silver shape. Suke-san walks at Ako-chan's right. He watches the Colonel watch Obia. Dango sniffs the cold, hard ice. Dalu and Gabu roll slowly in their Pods. Pen-san waddles close to Ako-chan, happy here. The cold gets into her coat, but she does not mind. The light moves on the ice, blue and white. Everything is still and clean and cold.

Ako-chan looks out at the far ice. It runs far away to the white sky. No path. No end. Just ice and light. She feels very small here. But she is not afraid. Her friends are all around her. Even the Colonel, hard and quiet, walks with them now. The far ice is cold. But today, they see it together.
