The ice is all around them now. The air is very cold. Ako-chan can see her breath. Inside the ship, Obia opens a big box. "It is cold outside," it says. "We need to stay warm. The cold land is close." Everyone comes to look. The box is full of warm things. They are soft and thick.

Obia gives Dango four small boots. Then a warm vest for his back. Dango shakes at first. Then he likes them. He is warm now. His tail wags fast. Obia gives Suke-san boots and a vest too. Suke-san sniffs them. He does not like new things. But he puts them on. "They are warm," he says. "Good." He stands tall in his new vest.

Ako-chan gets a coat from Obia. It is light but very warm. Then Obia works on the Pods. It makes them ready for the cold. The Pods grow bigger and warmer inside. "Now they keep the cold out," says Obia. Dalu runs into his Pod. "It is warm! So warm!" he says. Gabu steps into his Pod too. The red light and the blue light glow softly. The Pods are warm and safe now.

Everyone is ready now. Outside, the white land waits. Ako-chan looks out at the ice. It is cold and quiet and new. She holds Dango in his warm vest. Her friends stand close around her. They came so far to get here. Ako-chan is not afraid now. She is ready too. Now the cold land is right in front of them. The white world is huge and still. Ako-chan looks out at the cold white land.
