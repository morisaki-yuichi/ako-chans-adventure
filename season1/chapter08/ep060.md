The storm is gone. It is night now. The sea is calm and dark. The ship moves slowly south again. Ako-chan sits in her small room. Dango is there with her. It is just the two of them. The room is quiet and warm. A small lamp glows by the bed. Outside, the waves are soft now. Ako-chan is very tired. Her body still feels the storm.

She sits on the floor. Dango climbs into her lap. He is warm and heavy. She holds him close. She sits very still. She does not move at all. Then, slowly, she takes off her red glasses. She holds them in her hand. Without them, her face looks small. Her eyes are soft and bare. This is her true face. No one sees it but Dango.

Then the tears come. They are quiet. They fall down her face. She does not make a sound. Dango looks up at her. He feels her shake. He licks her cheek gently. "It is okay," she says to him. But her voice shakes too. She was so scared before. Now it all comes out. Her shoulders shake. The tears run warm. She holds Dango tight. He does not pull away. He stays right there in her arms.

Ako-chan holds Dango close. She lets the tears fall. No one sees her here. Only Dango. She does not need to be strong now. The red glasses rest in her hand. After a while, the tears stop. She is quiet again. Her face is calm. Her breath is slow and soft. She puts her glasses back on. Then she holds Dango until she sleeps.
