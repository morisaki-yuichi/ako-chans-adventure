Days pass. The storm is far behind them now. The sea is calm and grey. The air grows colder every day. One morning, Ako-chan looks out the window. She sees something far away. It floats on the sea. It is big and white and tall.

"Look!" she says. "What is that?" Everyone comes to the window. They look out together. It is ice. A huge wall of ice. It shines in the morning light. More of it floats near. Pale shapes rise on the grey sea. The first ice of the journey.

Daru climbs up to see. His eyes go wide. "Ice!" he says. "So much ice! Does it not melt?" Gabu looks out beside him. He is calm and still. "Here it does not," says Gabu. "Here it stays. It is too cold." Daru looks at the great white wall. He is quiet now. Even Daru is quiet.

The ice moves slowly past the ship. It is taller than the ship now. Up close, the ice is not just white. It is blue too, deep inside. It is bigger than anything she knows. The world is very quiet here. There are no birds. There are no waves. Only the soft sound of the cold sea. Ako-chan holds her breath and looks.

The ship moves past the ice. The ship is slow and careful now. Everyone watches the cold world come. Dango puts his paws on the glass. Suke-san stares out, ears up. Ako-chan feels the cold through the window. The white land is close now. Something new waits out there. Ako-chan looks out for a while. She does not look away.
