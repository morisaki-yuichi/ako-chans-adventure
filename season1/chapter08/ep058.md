The storm hits. The wind screams. Rain falls hard and cold. The waves rise like black hills. They crash over the ship. The ship tips far to one side. Everyone holds on tight. Ako-chan grips the rail. Her hands hurt. A huge wave comes. The ship leans more. It almost goes over.

Then, in the dark water, something rises. A huge black shape. Then another. Then more. They come up all around the ship. They are whales. Many whales. They make a wall around the ship. Their great bodies take the waves. The water hits them, not the ship. The ship is steady now. The whales hold the storm back with their bodies.

Inside the ship, her friends hold on. Dango lies flat on the floor. Suke-san digs his claws into the deck. Daru and Gabu stay deep in their Pods. Obia holds the wheel with strong paws. The ship rocks, but it stays up now.

Ako-chan looks down at the water. One whale is close to the ship. Its great eye finds her. She knows this eye. It is the whale from before. They helped it once. Now it helps them. "You came back," she says softly. The whale does not speak. It just holds its place. The waves break hard on its back. But it does not move away.

The whales make a great ring around the ship. The storm still screams above them. But the waves cannot reach the ship now. Ako-chan holds the rail and stares. She remembers the whale's words from before. "We will not forget," it said. Now she knows what they mean. Their dark backs shine in the rain. The ship is small inside their ring. The whales came back for them.
