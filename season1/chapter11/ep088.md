Now the Colonel comes. He walks in a straight line. His steps are even and sure. He stops in front of Ako-chan. He stands very straight and tall. His white eye-rings are bright in the sun. He looks right up at her. Pen-san waddles close behind him.

"Mission accomplished," says the Colonel. His voice is short and firm. Then he turns to Obia. For many days, he watched it. He did not trust it at all. He watched it work all day and night. He watched it walk out in the snow alone. Now he looks at it close. "You worked," he says. "You did not hide." He lowers his chin down low. It is a small thing. But it is his salute. From the Colonel, it means a lot.

Obia stands very still. Its blue eyes are soft and bright. It does not say a word. But it dips its head to the Colonel too. Ako-chan feels her throat go tight. Dango wags his tail slowly. Suke-san dips his head down too. Daru peeks out of his red Pod. Gabu looks on from his blue one. Pen-san stands close by the Colonel. The cold wind moves over them all. The ice shines brightly under the sun. No one speaks for a moment.

The Colonel stands up straight again. He stays here on the ice now. He guards his land and his small Emperor. Ako-chan gives him a low bow. "Thank you, Colonel," she says softly. He gives her one short nod. The two birds stand on the white ice. This is their home. This is their land. Ako-chan holds the moment close. That is all. From the Colonel, that is everything.
