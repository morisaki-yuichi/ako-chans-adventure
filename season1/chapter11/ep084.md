The lamp still glows. The night is quiet now. Obia sits with its head low. Its blue eyes are dim. No one speaks. The wind is soft outside. Ako-chan waits. She does not push it. She just waits, with her hand near. The others wait too.

Then Obia lifts its head. It looks at Ako-chan. It looks at all of them. "I will stop," it says. "Here. Now." The words are clear and strong. The blue light grows bright again. No one tells it to stop. No one gives it an order. Obia chooses. Itself. For the first time. Ako-chan sees how hard it is. The little silver one shakes, just once. Then it is still and sure.

Ako-chan feels something change. The low hum is gone now. The air in the room feels light. Dango lifts his head and looks up. Suke-san's ears come up tall. The Colonel watches, very still and quiet. Pen-san looks from face to face. Daru and Gabu peek out from their Pods. Outside, the snow lies quiet and white. The lamp light is warm and gold on every face. The night holds them all together.

"Okay," says Ako-chan softly. "We stop. Together." She puts her hand on Obia's head. Obia does not pull away. It is not the watcher now. It is one of them. The silver body is warm under her hand. Dango comes close and leans on Obia. For the first time, he does not mind. The room is quiet and warm. No one needs to speak now. They sit together in the soft lamp light. Something is over. And something new starts here.
