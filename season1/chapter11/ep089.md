The ship pulls away from the ice. Slowly, it moves out to sea. The cold water is grey and wide. Six friends stand on the deck. Ako-chan, Dango, and Obia. Daru and Gabu in their Pods. Suke-san at her right side. The ship hums low and deep.

Ako-chan looks back at the ice. Two small shapes stand there together. Pen-san and the Colonel. They grow smaller and smaller now. Pen-san waves one little wing. He jumps high on the ice. The Colonel stands straight and still. Ako-chan waves back with both hands. "Goodbye!" she calls. "I come back!" Her voice goes out over the cold sea. She watches them until she cannot see them. The white land falls away behind them.

Dango puts his paws on the rail. He looks back at the ice too. Daru is quiet in his red Pod. Gabu watches the land go, slow and calm. Suke-san sits close and still. The wind is cold but not wild now. The sea birds turn soft in the sky. The voices are quiet today. No one says a word for a while. Ako-chan thinks of Pen-san's warm body. She thinks of the Colonel's brave nod. Her heart is full and a little sad.

At last the white land is gone. There is only grey sea and pale sky. Ako-chan holds Dango close to her. She turns to face the north now. Obia stands beside her, not behind. The cold south is behind them now. The wide sea is all ahead. So many friends are far away now. But the long road home starts here. The grey waves lift the ship north.
