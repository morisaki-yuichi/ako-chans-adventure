The morning is bright and still. The ship waits by the ice. Today the friends go home. Pen-san walks slowly to Ako-chan. He knows it now. His small face is not playful today. He looks up at her with big dark eyes.

"Ako-chan," he says softly. "I grow up now." He stands up tall on the ice. "I will be a good Emperor." His voice shakes a little. Then he is small again. "You come back?" He runs to her and pushes into her arms. Ako-chan kneels down low and holds him close. His soft gray body is warm. She feels his small heart beat fast. "Yes, Pen-san," she says. "I come back. I promise." Pen-san pushes his head into her neck. He does not want to let go.

The cold wind moves over the ice. Dango stands quiet and watches them. He does not feel jealous now. He only looks a little sad. Daru and Gabu come close in their Pods. Suke-san sits at her right. The Colonel stands tall behind Pen-san. He watches his small friend, very still. Everyone is very quiet here. Ako-chan's eyes are wet. One tear falls and freezes on the ice.

At last, Pen-san steps back from her. He stands up straight and brave. But his eyes are full too. "Go now," he says. "I am okay." His small wings press hard to his sides. He tries to be big and strong. Ako-chan stands up slowly. She looks at him one more time. She wants to stay. But she cannot. The little Emperor stays on the ice. He watches her go, tall and small.
