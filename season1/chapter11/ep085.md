Later that night, Obia stands at the door. "Come," it says. "Look up." Ako-chan steps outside. The cold is sharp on her face. The others come out behind her. She looks up at the dark sky. And she stops still.

Green light moves across the dark. It flows like water over the sky. It is slow and soft. It is the aurora. The whole sky is alive now. Green and pale and bright. The light falls down on the white ice. It shines on the snow. It shines on all of them. No one says a word. Ako-chan watches it move. She never saw this before.

Their breath rises white in the cold. They stand close, all together. Dango stays pressed to Ako-chan's leg. Pen-san presses to her other side. Suke-san sits quiet at her right. Daru and Gabu look up from their Pods. The Colonel stands tall and still. The green light moves on Obia's silver face. Then the light turns soft pink. Then green again. The whole sky breathes slowly. Pen-san looks up with big round eyes. Daru is quiet for once. No one has words for this.

No one wants to go back inside. The cold does not matter now. They just watch the sky and stay close. Even the wind is quiet here. The night feels safe and whole.

Ako-chan does not speak. She does not need to. The light moves on, slow and green. Obia stands beside her now. Not behind her. Beside her. She looks at it, and it looks back. The cold land is full of quiet light. And for now, that is enough.
