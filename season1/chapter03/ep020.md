The rocks run along the high cliff. Far below, the sea moves and shines. The waves crash on the stone below. Daru explores the warm white rocks. He looks under every stone. He is so curious. He never stays in one place. Ako-chan tries to keep him close. Obia stands near the edge. Dango walks beside the little tortoise.

Then Daru sees something new. A small white bird sits near the edge. Daru runs to see it. "What is that? Let me see!" The bird flies away. But Daru runs on. He is already there. He is at the very edge! Ako-chan's heart jumps. "Daru! Stop!" But Dango is faster. He runs to Daru. He puts his body in the way. He pushes the little tortoise back. He does not bark. He does not stop pushing. He moves Daru away from the edge.

The cliff goes straight down to the sea. The water is far below. Cold air comes up from the drop. Ako-chan runs and picks up Daru. She holds him close to her chest. Her hands shake a little. Her heart beats hard and fast. She is so afraid for him. Daru does not understand. "What? What is down there?" he says. He wants to look again. Dango stands between Daru and the edge.

Ako-chan holds Daru tight. "You must be careful," she says. "Please." Daru looks up at her. Then he looks at Dango. The big dog is still close. He did not wait. He did not think. He just moved. Daru does not pull away now. He is quiet, just for a moment. Something passes between the dog and the tortoise.
