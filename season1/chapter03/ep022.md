Obia works all morning on the rocks. It is busy and quiet. Then it stops. On the stone sits something round. It is clear, like a small ball. Daru runs up to see it. "What is that? What is that?" "For you," says Obia. "It is warm inside."

Daru looks at the clear ball. He is not sure. He walks around it slowly. Then he steps inside. It is soft and warm in there. Daru takes one step. The ball rolls a little. He stops. It stops. Daru takes another step. It rolls again! Now Daru understands. He walks, and it moves. He walks faster, and it goes faster. Then he runs! The ball rolls fast across the rocks. It glows a bright red. Daru laughs and rolls along. "Look at me! Look at me!" says Daru.

Ako-chan laughs and claps her hands. The glow shines red on the white stone. Obia watches the little tortoise roll. "It is a Pod," Obia says. "It keeps you safe and warm." Dango sniffs the rolling Pod. He jumps back when it moves. Then he sniffs it again. Daru rolls past him, happy and fast. Dango barks once and runs after. The Pod is quick and bright. It rolls like a little sun.

At last Daru stops. He sits inside the soft Pod. His small eyes are sleepy now. The Pod glows red and soft around him. He is safe. He is warm. He can go anywhere now. The waves wash softly on the rocks. The day is calm and gold. Ako-chan looks at her four friends. The long road south does not feel so hard.
