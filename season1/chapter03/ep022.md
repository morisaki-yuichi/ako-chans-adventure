Obia works all morning on the rocks. It is busy and quiet. Then it stops. On the stone sits something round. It is clear, like a small ball. Dalu runs up to see it. "What is that? What is that?" "For you," says Obia. "It is warm inside."

Dalu looks at the clear ball. He is not sure. He walks around it slowly. Then he steps inside. It is soft and warm in there. Dalu takes one step. The ball rolls a little. He stops. It stops. Dalu takes another step. It rolls again! Now Dalu understands. He walks, and it moves. He walks faster, and it goes faster. Then he runs! The ball rolls fast across the rocks. It glows a bright red. Dalu laughs and rolls along. "Look at me! Look at me!" says Dalu.

Ako-chan laughs and claps her hands. The glow shines red on the white stone. Obia watches the little tortoise roll. "It is a Pod," Obia says. "It keeps you safe and warm." Dango sniffs the rolling Pod. He jumps back when it moves. Then he sniffs it again. Dalu rolls past him, happy and fast. Dango barks once and runs after. The Pod is quick and bright. It rolls like a little sun.

At last Dalu stops. He sits inside the soft Pod. His small eyes are sleepy now. The Pod glows red and soft around him. He is safe. He is warm. He can go anywhere now. The waves wash softly on the rocks. The day is calm and gold. Ako-chan looks at her three friends. The long road south does not feel so hard.
