Ako-chan waits by the rock. The sun is hot on her back. The dark crack is quiet and still. The sea shines brightly behind the rocks. Salt and heat fill the warm air. Then something changes. The small sound comes again. Tap, tap, tap. The sound comes faster now.

Then it bursts out! A small round thing shoots from the crack. It runs fast across the rocks. Ako-chan jumps back in surprise. It is a baby tortoise! His shell is small and round. It is yellow-brown with black lines. He is no bigger than her hand. He spins in a quick circle. "What is that? What is that?" he says. "Let me see! Let me see!" He runs to Dango. He runs to Obia. He runs back to Ako-chan's feet. "Hello! Hello! I am Daru!" "Who are you? Where do you go?"

Dango steps back fast. His ears go flat in surprise. He never saw a thing so quick. Obia watches the small tortoise. Its pale eyes follow every move. Daru does not stop. His little legs run and run. He climbs a little rock. He looks out at the sea. "Wow! Big water! So big!" Then he comes down again. He cannot stay still for a moment.

Ako-chan laughs softly. She kneels down on the warm rock. "Hello, Daru," she says. "I am Ako-chan." The little tortoise looks up at her. His small eyes are bright and quick. He is not afraid at all. He is only curious. He makes one more happy circle. His small feet tap fast on the stone. The white rocks are not quiet now.
