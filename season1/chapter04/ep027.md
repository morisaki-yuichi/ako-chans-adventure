The day grows late in the desert. The sun sits low and red. Long shadows fall from the tall rocks. The heat is softer now than before. Ako-chan walks among the big stones. Dango walks beside her in his boots. Daru rolls along in his Pod. The sand is quiet under their feet.

Then Ako-chan stops. Something moves by a low rock. It is small and round and still. It is the same colour as the stone. She looks hard at it. For a moment it does not move. Then it moves once. And then it is gone. "Did you see that?" she says. But the rock is empty now. There is only sand and shadow.

Dango runs to the rock fast. He puts his nose to the ground. He sniffs hard all around the stone. His tail goes stiff and high. He follows a trail in the sand. But the trail stops at the rocks. He cannot find the small thing. He looks back at Ako-chan. He makes a low, soft sound.

The shadows grow longer on the sand. The red rocks turn dark and quiet. The desert holds its breath around them. Daru rolls up to the rock too. "What was it? What was it?" he says. He looks under the stone with big eyes. But there is nothing there now.

Ako-chan does not know what it was. But she is sure of one thing. Something is there in the rocks. Something small and quiet, watching them. It saw them. And then it hid. She looks at the still red stones. The desert keeps its small secret. Someone is out there.
