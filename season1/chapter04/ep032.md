The sun is warm over the camp now. Obia works on something new. It works quietly with its tools. Soon a round, clear Pod sits on the sand. "For you, Gabu," says Obia. "It is warm and safe inside." Gabu looks at the Pod for a while.

Gabu walks up to the clear Pod slowly. He studies it with his calm eyes. Then he steps inside, slowly and carefully. He takes one step. The Pod rolls a little. He takes another step. It rolls again. Gabu walks at a steady, even pace. The Pod moves smoothly across the sand. It does not rush like Dalu's. It glows a soft blue.

Dalu rolls up fast in his red Pod. "Look! Look! Yours is blue!" he says. The two Pods sit side by side. One glows red. One glows blue. Dalu's Pod is quick and bright. Gabu's Pod is calm and steady. Dango watches the blue light roll. He does not bark this time.

The blue Pod rolls in an even line. It does not turn or stop fast. It keeps one calm and steady beat. Gabu walks inside at his gentle pace. His small feet keep a soft rhythm. The Pod hums quietly on the sand. It is just right for him. It moves the way Gabu moves.

Gabu moves at his own pace now. He does not go fast like Dalu. He does not need to. The blue Pod rolls slowly and steadily. Inside, Gabu is warm and calm. Ako-chan looks at her two small friends. Red and blue, side by side. Each one goes his own way. And that is just fine.
