A new day comes warm and bright. The desert is quiet in the morning. Daru waits on the sand beside Ako-chan. Today he does not run. He keeps very still and quiet. The small tortoise is there once more. It watches them from the rocks. Then, slowly, it starts to come.

The tortoise takes one step on the sand. Then it stops and looks around. It looks at Ako-chan for a while. Then it takes one more step. Step by step, it comes closer. It stops. It looks. It walks again. It comes nearer than yesterday now. But then it stops once more. It does not come all the way.

The morning light is soft and gold. It falls warm on the red sand. The tortoise is so close now. Ako-chan can see its yellow shell. She can see the black marks on its back. Its small head turns towards her slowly. Its eyes are calm and dark and kind. It is thinking. It is slow and sure.

The desert is very still and quiet. Only the tortoise's feet make a sound. They scratch softly on the warm sand. Daru watches with big round eyes. He holds his breath this time. He stretches his neck out far. He wants it to come so much. Dango sits close by his side.

The tortoise stops a few steps away. It is closer than ever before today. But it is not ready yet. Ako-chan does not reach for it. She just smiles and stays still. She thinks of how it began. First only a shape. Then five metres away. Then one step on the sand. Each day it comes a little closer. Beside her, even Daru holds still now. The fast one is learning to wait.
