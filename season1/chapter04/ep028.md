Morning comes early to the desert. The air is still cool for now. The red rocks glow soft and pink. Ako-chan walks out among the stones. Dango walks close beside her. Daru comes too, out on the warm sand. They look for the small thing from last night.

Then Ako-chan sees it. On a low rock sits a small tortoise. It is about five metres away. Its shell is yellow with black marks. It looks straight at Ako-chan. It does not run away. It does not come closer. It just sits and watches them all. Ako-chan stops where she is. "Hello," she says very softly. "We see you." The tortoise does not move. So they wait. They wait for a long time.

The sun climbs higher in the sky. It heats the red rocks fast. The air shakes above the hot ground. Daru wants to go and see. "Let me look! Let me look!" he says. He starts to walk forward fast. But Dango steps in his way. Dango leans on Daru's shell. He holds him still with his body. Obia stands and watches too.

The small tortoise watches them with care. It looks at Ako-chan. Then it looks at Dango. Its dark eyes move slowly and calmly. It studies each face for a long time. This one is not like Daru at all. It does not rush. It only watches and waits.

An hour goes by in the heat. The small tortoise is still there. It is still five metres away. It does not come to them. But it does not leave. Ako-chan smiles just a little. It wants to see them too. That is enough for now.
