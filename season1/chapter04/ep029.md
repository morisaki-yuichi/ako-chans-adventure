The sun is high now over the desert. The heat sits heavy on the sand. The small tortoise waits on its rock. It still keeps its careful distance. Ako-chan moves very slowly toward it. Then she stops a few steps away. She does not want to scare it.

Ako-chan sits down on the hot sand. She moves slowly and stays low. She puts her hand flat on the ground. The sand is hot under her palm. She does not reach for the tortoise. She just waits, with her hand open. The tortoise looks at her hand. It looks at her face. For a long moment, nothing moves. Then the tortoise takes one step. One small step toward Ako-chan.

No one moves at all. Dango holds his breath beside her. He keeps close to Daru on the sand. Daru wants to run to it. But he stays still this time. The hot sand burns in Ako-chan's hand. She does not move it away. A small wind lifts the red dust. Then it falls quiet again.

The tortoise keeps its eyes on her hand. It does not trust her fully yet. But it does not turn away now. Ako-chan breathes slowly and stays soft. The sun is hot on her neck and back. Sweat runs down beside her glasses. Still she does not move her hand.

The tortoise stops after one step. It does not come any closer now. But it is nearer than before. Ako-chan keeps her hand on the sand. She waits, calm and quiet, in the heat. One step is very small. But it is a start. She can be patient for this.
