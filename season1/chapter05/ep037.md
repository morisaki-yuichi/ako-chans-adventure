The ferry moves over the calm sea. Far ahead, green hills come into view. The air is warm and very wet. It feels soft and heavy on the skin. This is a new kind of green. Ako-chan sits on the deck to rest. The long road is behind them now.

Dalu and Gabu come out of their Pods. They sit on the warm deck together. Dalu looks at the far hills. "That green is different!" he says. "It is not like home. Not like the desert." Gabu looks at it quietly. "Yes," he says at last. "It is darker. And more wet." Dalu runs in a small happy circle. Gabu just watches the slow green land.

The boat rocks gently on the waves. Its low sound fills the still air. Dalu slows down and stops his circle. He sits down close beside Gabu. The two Pods rest near them, side by side. Red and blue, calm in the sun. Far off, the coast grows near. The smell of land comes over the water.

Everyone is very tired now. But they are here, all together. Dango lies down by Ako-chan's leg. He puts his head upon her knee. His eyes are heavy and soft now. Ako-chan rests her hand on his back. Obia stands near them at the rail. Its eyes are pale and blue.

The hills come slowly closer now. The air smells of leaves and sea. No one says very much right now. They do not need to speak at all. The long road made them one group. Ako-chan looks at her tired friends. They are almost there. Almost to Japan.
