The sun moves low across the sky. The giant tortoise stands on the rock. Then, all at once, it stops. Its head lifts up high above. Its old eyes go wide and still. It does not move. It does not blink. The great tortoise just looks straight up.

"What is it?" Ako-chan asks softly. "What do you see?" But the giant does not answer her. It only stares up at the empty sky. Ako-chan looks up too. There is nothing there. Just pale blue, far and high. No clouds. No birds. Nothing at all. But the giant cannot look away from it.

The hours pass slowly on the hot island. The giant does not move one bit. It stands like stone and looks at the sky. Dalu and Gabu watch with wide, scared eyes. Dango huddles close to Ako-chan's leg. Suke-san stares to the south and growls low. He feels it too. Something pulls hard from down there.

The sun sinks lower behind the hills. Yet the giant looks up high. Its great body does not move at all. It is like a grey stone on the rock. Dalu rolls back into his Pod, afraid. Gabu stays close beside the frozen giant. The cold comes slowly into the evening air.

Ako-chan knows this feeling now. It is the wrong thing. The same wrong from far away. It is stronger here than ever before. The south is very close now. She looks out past the black rocks. The sea waits, grey and wide and deep. Whatever is wrong, it is down there. And they go that way next. She does not know what waits down there. But something does. Ako-chan holds Dango close and stays still.
