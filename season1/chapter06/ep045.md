The ship is ready at last. It stands tall and strong by the dock. Fresh wood shines in the morning sun. New white sails hang clean and bright. The grey old ship is gone now. A real ship waits in its place. The sea is wide and blue ahead.

One by one, they go on board. Ako-chan walks up first. Dango runs up close behind her. Daru and Gabu roll up in their Pods. Suke-san comes up after them, quiet and sure. Obia steps on last and looks to the south. Then the ship moves slowly from the dock. It turns its nose toward the open sea. The sails fill with a warm wind from behind. The little port grows small behind them. Now they are six on board. Ako-chan, Dango, Obia, Daru, Gabu, and Suke-san. Six friends on one ship, going south.

Dango wants the place at Ako-chan's right. But Suke-san is already there. He stands at her right side, a little behind. Dango pushes in and gives him a look. Suke-san does not move one bit. So Dango takes her left side instead. He leans hard on her left leg. Ako-chan laughs down at the two dogs. She puts one hand on each head. "There is room for both of you," she says. Daru watches from his red Pod. "Two dogs now!" he says. Gabu just rolls quietly along beside him.

The green hills of Japan grow small. The wide sea opens up ahead. Ako-chan looks out at the long way south. Six of them stand on the deck together. The wind is warm. The sails are full. Ako-chan is not afraid now. Her friends are all close beside her. Whatever waits out there, they go to meet it. Together.
