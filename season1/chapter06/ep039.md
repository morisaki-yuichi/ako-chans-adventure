The morning is bright over the port. High rocks rise above the grey water. Pine trees grow along the top of them. The air smells of pine and warm earth. Ako-chan looks up at the high rocks. Dango stands very close beside her. He looks up too. He does not bark.

A small dog sits on the highest rock. It is red-brown, with white socks. Its face is sharp, just like a fox. Its ears stand straight up. Its tail curls over its back. It does not move at all. Its eyes look down at Dango first. Then they move slowly to Ako-chan. Then they find Obia. And there they stop. The eyes stay on Obia a long time. The small dog watches Obia. Its eyes are slow and careful.

Daru rolls forward in his red Pod. "Who is that? Who is that?" he says. But then he sees the sharp eyes too. He goes quiet and very still. Gabu stops beside him in his blue Pod. Gabu watches the small dog watch Obia. No one moves now. The smell of pine and earth sits heavy in the warm air. A single bird calls far away. Even Dango holds still beside her. He does not sniff at the rocks. He only looks up at the watching dog.

Obia stands very still under those eyes. It does not look away. The small dog does not come down. It does not run away. It just sits high up and watches. Ako-chan feels something new here. This dog is not like Daru. It is not like Gabu. Something sharp is in its eyes. Something careful. Something that waits.
