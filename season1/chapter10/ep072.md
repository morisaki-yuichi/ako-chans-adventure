Deep night lies over the white land. The ship rests against the ice. The sky has no moon tonight. Only the stars hang over the world. No bird calls in the dark. Inside, everyone sleeps. Dango sleeps at Ako-chan's feet. Pen-san sleeps near them, warm in the group. Outside, the cold is hard and still.

Then a small sound. The ship door opens slowly. Cold air slips through the gap. Obia steps out onto the ice alone. It does not wake anyone. Snow crunches softly under its feet. Obia walks a few steps from the ship. Then it stops. It stands very still. It faces the south, into the dark. Its eyes shine pale blue. They are brighter than before. A low hum comes from it, soft and deep. The same shape. The same sound. Again, like before.

Up on the ice, a dark shape watches. It is the Colonel. He came up here in the dark. He stands on a high block of ice. He does not move at all. His round eyes are fixed on Obia. He sees the pale blue light. He hears the low hum. He does not call out. He sees Obia face the south. Snow falls slowly between them. The thin wind bites at the ice. The Colonel watches every moment.

At last the hum stops. Obia's eyes go dim. It turns and walks back to the ship. The door closes softly behind it. Inside, no one wakes. Outside, the Colonel still stands on the ice. He looks into the dark. He sees only black and cold. He does not sleep tonight. He keeps his watch alone in the cold.
