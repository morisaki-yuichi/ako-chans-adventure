The dogs stop at a low hill of snow. Dango lifts his head up. He makes a small, sad sound. Suke-san stands very still beside him. Ako-chan comes up fast behind them. Then she sees it. And her heart stops. Snow covers the wide land all around. The world is still and silent here.

There, in the deep snow, is Obia. Snow lies thick over its silver body. Only part of it shows now. Ako-chan falls to her knees. She pushes the cold snow away. Her hands shake in the freezing air. Obia does not move at all. It is hard and cold as the ice. Its blue eyes are dark now. The light in them is gone. "Obia," she says. But Obia does not answer. She shakes it gently in the snow. Nothing moves. Obia is cold and still.

No one can say a word. The white plain is wide and silent. The wind is gone now. Dango pushes his nose at Obia. He does not stop. Suke-san lies down in the snow. Pen-san stands very still and small. Up on the snow, the Colonel watches. He says nothing at all. He only looks down at Obia. The snow falls softly and slowly again. The air is sharp and very cold.

Ako-chan brushes the last snow away. Obia lies still in her arms now. So cold. So quiet. So far away. The silver body does not shine. The dark eyes do not light. The friends stand around them in the snow. No one moves. No one speaks. Ako-chan holds Obia in her arms. She cannot believe her eyes. The cold land holds them all.
