The red collar still sits on the floor. The storm still beats on the ship. Outside, the wind does not stop. No one knows what to do now. Then Suke-san stands up tall. He looks at the red collar. He looks at Ako-chan. His eyes are clear and sure.

"I trust Obia," says Suke-san. The room goes quiet at his words. Suke-san was afraid of Obia before. He watched it. He growled at it. But not now. "Obia left this for us," he says. "Obia is one of us. I trust Obia." The Colonel stares at him hard. "You believe in it?" he says. "I do." Suke-san does not look away from him. His voice is low but very sure.

Then Suke-san turns to the door. "I am going out," he says. "Obia needs us now." He takes a step towards the door. Ako-chan stands up fast too. "I am going too," she says. Dango jumps up beside her, ready. He scratches at the heavy door. He wants out into the white storm. Pen-san watches with wide, dark eyes. The cold air sits at the door.

But the Colonel steps in front of them. "No," he says. "Not now. Listen." The storm still screams outside the ship. "You cannot see out there," he says. "The compass is broken. There is no way. You go out, you get lost too." Ako-chan stops. She knows he is right. Suke-san stops by the door. Ako-chan looks at the dark door. Her friend is out there alone. The snow storm has no end tonight. The wait is very hard. But they cannot go out into the storm.
