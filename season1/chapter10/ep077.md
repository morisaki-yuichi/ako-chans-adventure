Then the snow storm hits the land. The world turns white in a moment. Ako-chan cannot see the ice. She cannot see the sky at all. White is everywhere, thick and fast. The wind screams across the white land. "Inside! Everyone inside!" calls Ako-chan.

They all run for the ship door. Dango runs close to Ako-chan's side. Suke-san pushes Daru and Gabu along. Pen-san waddles fast behind them all. The Colonel comes in last of all. Ako-chan pulls the heavy door shut. It closes with a loud, hard bang. The roar of the storm goes behind the wall. Inside, it is dark and close. Everyone breathes fast and hard. The door holds the wild storm out.

Daru and Gabu shake at Ako-chan's feet. Their small legs cannot keep still. Ako-chan rubs Daru's small shell. She rubs Gabu's shell too, softly and slowly. Pen-san is afraid too. He leans hard on Ako-chan's side. She holds him close and warm. Dango still holds the red collar tight. Suke-san stands close by the door. His ears point at the loud wall. The whole ship shakes in the wind. Snow hits the dark windows hard. The wind howls without stop outside. The dark room feels small and tight.

Then Ako-chan looks around the room. She looks for everyone in the dark. Dango. Suke-san. Daru. Gabu. Pen-san. The Colonel too. But one is not here. "Obia?" she calls out. No answer comes back. "Obia!" Still nothing. Her heart goes cold inside. She looks at the shaking door. Obia is out in the white storm. Alone. Out in all that white and cold. Obia is gone. And no one knows why.
