Ako-chan holds Obia close to her. The silver body is cold and still. She does not let it go. Her friends stand quiet in the snow. The white land is wide around them. No one moves at all. The air is cold and very still. Snow rests on Obia's silver face.

"Obia," says Ako-chan, softly and low. "Please come back. Please." Her arms hold the still body close. She holds Obia tight to her chest. Her tears fall down on its face. One tear. Then more. They fall on the cold silver. She holds Obia and she cries. The snow is silent all around. Her tears keep falling on Obia. She cannot stop them now. "I am here," she says. "I am here."

Then something changes in her arms. Slowly, a small light comes. Deep in Obia's eyes, blue light. It is faint at first. Then it grows. The pale blue light is soft and warm. The dark eyes are dark no more. Obia's eyes open slowly. They find Ako-chan's face. "Obia!" she says. Her voice breaks. Dango barks and jumps beside them. Suke-san stands up tall in the snow. Pen-san makes a happy, small sound. The Colonel watches, still and quiet.

Ako-chan laughs and cries at once. She holds Obia close and warm. Then she picks up the red collar. She puts it back around Obia's neck. It is where it should be now. The collar is back with Obia again. Obia looks up at her, bright and clear. The friends come close in the snow. Ako-chan holds Obia to her chest. Warm tears fall on the red collar. No one needs to say a word. The long night is over now.
