The next day the sky changes fast. Dark clouds roll in from the south. The wind grows loud and cold. The air feels heavy and strange. Ako-chan stands by the ship with Obia. Obia stands very still. It reads the cold air with its sensors. Something big is on the way.

"The cold falls fast," says Obia. "The wind rises." Its blue eyes turn to the south. "A big storm comes. Bigger than before." Ako-chan takes out her compass. She holds it up. The needle spins round and round. It cannot find the way. "The compass is wrong now," says Obia. Ako-chan looks at the grey sky. Then the Colonel comes fast across the ice. He looks up at the clouds. He knows this sky well. "It is a snow storm," he says. "The worst kind." His voice is hard and low.

Pen-san stands near Ako-chan. He always plays and laughs. But not now. His small face is still and serious. For the first time, he looks afraid. Dango wedges close to her boots. Suke-san's fur stands up high. Daru and Gabu go quiet in their Pods. The wind pulls hard at Ako-chan's coat. Snow starts to fly past them all. The light grows dim in the day. Far off, the sky turns black. The cold bites deep through her coat. The ship creaks against the hard ice.

Ako-chan looks back at the ship. Then she looks at the dark sky. The storm is close now. Very close. Obia stands still beside her. Its blue eyes watch the clouds. No one says a word. Everyone stands closer together now. The cold wind fills the white land. The white land waits for the storm. Something is coming. And there is little time left.
