Morning comes grey and cold. The light is flat and pale. Frost sits on the ship's deck. The group stands outside on the ice. Ako-chan is there with Dango. Suke-san, Daru, Gabu, and Pen-san too. Obia stands a little apart. Then the Colonel walks up to them. His steps are hard and straight. No one speaks at first.

The Colonel stops in front of Obia. His round eyes are sharp and hard. "Last night," he says. His voice is hard and clear. "I saw Obia. Outside. Alone." He waits a moment. "What was it doing?" Everyone goes quiet on the ice. Ako-chan's heart beats fast and hard. She looks at Obia. Obia does not move. It does not answer him. "Tell me," says the Colonel. Still Obia says nothing at all. Its blue eyes are calm and far.

Suke-san stands close to Ako-chan. A low growl sits in his throat. His ears point straight up now. Dango presses hard on her leg. Daru and Gabu pull into their shells. Pen-san looks from face to face. He does not understand the dark mood. The whole group waits in silence. The cold air bites at every face. Snow drifts past in slow lines. No one says a word.

The Colonel does not look away. "I am watching it," he says. Then he turns and walks off. Snow falls light on Ako-chan's coat. She stays very still and quiet. She thinks of the train, far behind. She thinks of the ship at sea. Her chest feels tight again. The ice is silent all around. This is not the first time. And she knows that now.
