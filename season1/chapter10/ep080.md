The snow storm stops in the night. Morning comes white and still. Ako-chan opens the heavy door. Cold, clean air comes in. The whole land is deep in snow. Everything is soft and new and quiet. The sky is pale and clear now. Snow lies thick on the ship and ice.

"We can go now," says Ako-chan. They all step out onto the snow. Dango holds the red collar still. He takes a long, deep smell of it. It carries Obia's smell. Then his nose goes to the ground. He starts to walk, slowly and surely. Suke-san walks close beside him. His nose works at the cold air too. "This way," says Suke-san. The two dogs follow the faint trail. Dango's nose never leaves the snow. They move slowly across the deep snow. The trail is hard to hold here.

The Colonel walks out in front of them. He knows the ice and snow well. He picks the safe way for them. But he still does not trust Obia. He comes to see, not to believe. Pen-san waddles close behind Ako-chan. He wants to help his friend too. Daru and Gabu stay warm in their Pods. The snow is deep under everyone's feet. The bright sun shines on the ice. It hurts Ako-chan's eyes a little.

Ako-chan watches Dango's small back. He moves on through the white land. Nose down. Tail low. Step by step. He follows the smell of Obia. The others follow close behind him. No one speaks out on the snow. They only hope to find their friend. Her heart beats with hope and fear. Please be safe, she thinks. Please.
