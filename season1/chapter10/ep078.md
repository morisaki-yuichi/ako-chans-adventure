The storm still roars outside the ship. Night sits dark and heavy on them. Obia is still out there in the cold. Ako-chan goes to the door and looks out. She wants to run and find it. But outside is only white. She cannot see the ice. She cannot see one step ahead. So she stays inside, close and warm. No one can sleep tonight. They all think of Obia. They cannot help it now. They can only wait. So they begin to talk about it.

"Obia helped us many times," says Ako-chan. "It made our warm clothes. It found the way." Suke-san looks down at the floor. "But it kept secrets too," he says. "Those strange nights. The hum. The south." The Colonel stands by the wall. "I do not trust it," he says. "I saw it that night." Both things are true. Obia helped them. And Obia hid things. The room feels quiet and unsure. No one is sure what it means.

Then Dango stands up slowly. He walks to the middle of the room. The red collar is in his mouth. He puts it down on the floor. Everyone looks at the small red collar. The fire light shines on it. Daru and Gabu come close to look. Pen-san leans in to see it too. They all know this red collar well. Ako-chan kneels down beside Dango. She listens to his quiet voice. The others wait and watch them.

"Obia gave this to Dango," she says. "Just before the storm came." The room goes very still now. "Obia knew something," says Ako-chan softly. "It gave the collar away. Then it went out alone." No one speaks for a long moment. Even the Colonel says nothing now. He just looks down at it. Obia left this behind for them. The little red collar feels very heavy.
