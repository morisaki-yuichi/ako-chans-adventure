**前提**: Kara-sanはv2 Ep207（To the Park）で確立済み（Ako-chan付与名・Pen-san方式）。本話はCh26＝冬・Ako-chan POV・少年とは未接触（見ない・話しかけない）。現217の「二つの世界」の締めは維持し、**地の文で要約されていた声（"The crow speaks of seeds..."）を直接話法で演じ直す**のが本題。

<!-- body-A-start -->
Winter holds the park quiet and bright. Ako-chan sits on the bench with her friends. The bare trees stand black against a pale sky. Frost hides all day in the shaded grass. Dango rests his warm chin on her boot. The three Pods glow on the path — red, blue, yellow.

A heavy flap comes down through the cold air. Kara-san lands on the back of the bench. He is the oldest crow in the park, and he carries himself like it. "Cold today," he says, rough and plain. "The ground is hard. Slow worms. But the pond people bring more bread."

"Good morning, Kara-san," says Ako-chan.

"A good one, yes. Quiet sky. Nothing wrong." He turns one bright eye towards the pond. "The ducks will complain. They always do."

From the water, a duck calls, low and flat. "The crow takes too much bread. Every day he takes too much."

Kara-san clicks his beak. "The crow is quick. That is all." A squirrel runs down the frosted trunk and stops. "Mine," it says, quick and high. "My tree. My store. Mine." Then it is gone, up into the branches.

Ako-chan laughs, soft and white in the winter air. She listens with her eyes half closed, the way she has listened since she was small. Every small voice is calm. The park under the pale sky is at peace. Dango's ear turns at the crow, then settles. Suke-san watches from her right, easy and still. Luca's head is out in the thin sun.

Far across the park, the boy sits on his bench. He looks at the same trees, the same birds. But no voice reaches him on the wind. He does not know the park can speak. Two worlds share one quiet afternoon. One is full of voices. The other is silent. And neither one knows the other yet.
<!-- body-A-end -->

**執筆メモ**: ①冒頭文は現217の開幕を保存。②Kara-sanの register＝ぶっきらぼう・実利（虫とパン）・少し偉そう。鴨との小競り合いで軽さ（方針5の「軽」）。③Ako-chanに笑いと発話を返す（S3で彼女の肉声が痩せている問題への処方）。④「二つの世界」の対比は現行の良さをそのまま。grey不使用のまま少年の無音側を描けることの実証。

## Pilot B — v2 Ep227 **The Witnesses**

```yaml

```

**前提**: v2 Ep212（Watched in Return改稿）でGabuが「...Too still, for a young one. I will wait.」と留保済み。本話はその**評決の合流点**＝Gabuが数週間の観察の末に自分の言葉を与える。🔒逐語（Suke-san・Dalu・Gabuの現236台詞）はすべて保存。Dangoは所作のみ。Ch28＝晩冬・雪解けの兆しのみ（「春」と明言しない）。

<!-- body-B-start -->
The afternoon is calm, and the park is quiet. The long frost is loosening at last. Water drips slowly from the bare branches. Ako-chan sits on the bench with her friends. The boy is on his bench, far across the grass. He watches them, as he does every day. But today, the watching runs both ways.

Suke-san sits to her right, ears up and soft. "He comes each day," he says, low and sure. "No bad feeling. He only wants to see." Dalu leans forward in his bright red Pod. "I like him," Dalu says. "He stays so still!" From his blue Pod, Gabu watches for a long time. For weeks he has held his word back. Now he gives it, slow and calm. "...He is alone," Gabu says. "But not unkind." Luca says nothing at all. She only turns her small head towards the far bench, and keeps it there. That is her whole answer.

Ako-chan hears them, each quiet voice in turn. She listens, still, and does not speak. Dango lies against her foot, warm and heavy. His tail moves once across the grass. The thin sound of water runs under everything. The Pods glow soft against the wet ground. Four small watchers face the lonely bench, and their eyes are not sharp, and not cold.

Far away, the boy is watching her too. Her head turns slowly from one friend to the next. To him it looks like listening — like a quiet talk he cannot hear. Today, in small voices, they have chosen him. He knows nothing of it. He only feels that the afternoon is a little warmer. The animals keep their quiet watch, and wait.
<!-- body-B-end -->

**執筆メモ**: ①現236の🔒台詞は一語も変えていない。②新規は3点のみ——Gabuの「留保→評決」フレーム（For weeks he has held his word back＝現在完了のB1示範）、Lucaの無言の行動（That is her whole answer）、少年側1カット（To him it looks like listening＝観察スレッド、greyなしで少年知覚を書ける実証）。③評決が「出来事」になったので、この回に初めて賭け金と決着がある。④雪解けの音（water drips / thin sound of water）で季節を1段進める（方針5の季節循環）。

## Pilot C — v2 Ep277 **The Two Old Soldiers**

**前提**: v2 Ep275-276（現286-287＝固まる・誰にも受け取られない理解）の**当夜**。二羽が初めて言葉を交わす回＝Ch33「声のある章」の開幕。抑制・静かなbittersweet（涙・嘆きなし＝2026-06-27確定トーンの維持）。ロボットは「it / the friend / the small silver one」・名前なし。ループの説明はしない（「理解しようとしない」で276と接続）。**「It does not know us yet.」はv2 Ep282の🔒「You came back. But you do not know us yet.」への意図的な前echo**。ロボットはこの時点で二羽の声を解さない（理解の獲得はEp290）。

<!-- body-C-start -->
Night settles into the ice cave. The boy sleeps by his tools, deep inside his coat. Beside him rests the small silver one. Its blue-white eyes glow low, like a lamp before sleep. The glow lies soft on the old blue ice. At the far wall stand two birds, side by side. All day, neither of them has spoken.

Pen-san breaks the long silence first. "You saw it too," he says, very low.

"I saw it," says the General.

"The collar. The eyes. The sound when it walks."

"Yes."

"It is the friend," says Pen-san. His voice shakes once, then holds. "It came back to us."

The General is quiet for a while. "It does not know us," he says at last. "It looked at me, and nothing moved. Ice looks at us that way."

"Not yet," says Pen-san. "It does not know us yet."

They do not try to understand how this can be. The thought is too big, and they let it stand. Once, in another life, the two of them crossed the white land beside that collar. The cave ticks and creaks around them, slow and old. The boy's breath rises and falls, small in the great dark. The blue-white glow dims and brightens, then dims again. The two birds keep their voices low, out of old habit. No one in this cave could understand them anyway. They lower their voices all the same.

"What do we do?" asks Pen-san.

The General looks at the sleeping boy. Then he looks at the soft blue glow. "We stay," he says. "We watch."

"Yes. Like before."

The knowing is still heavy in them both. But a weight grows lighter when two stand under it. The two old soldiers settle into the dark, side by side. In the deep south, in the ice, a new watch begins.
<!-- body-C-end -->

**執筆メモ**: ①v1台帳の「Ep292 Adelie=台詞なし」はv2承認（ペンギン対話劇）が上書き——ただしAdelieの register は命令形・短文で保存（「We stay.
