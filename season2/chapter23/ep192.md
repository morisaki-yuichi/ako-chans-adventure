Many days pass over the wide sea. Then, one morning, something changes. Far ahead, under the low clouds, there is land. A long green coast appears. It is England. It is home. Ako-chan's heart beats fast. They left this place three years ago. Now, at last, they are coming back.

The flying boat flies lower and lower. The green land grows close. Ako-chan can see fields and trees. She can see small gray towns. The sea meets the shore in a white line. Soon she will see her own garden. Soon they will all be home. Ako-chan holds the controls with steady hands. She points the nose down toward the coast.

Dango stands up and wags his tail. He knows this smell, even from the air. Suke-san looks hard through the window. Daru and Gabu press close to watch. Ruka stays calm in her small Pod. Ako-chan opens a window just a little. The air smells of rain and grass. It is the smell of England. It is the smell of home. The friends are quiet, but their eyes are bright. Their long journey is almost over now. After everything, they are nearly there.

Ako-chan looks at the land below. Her heart is full of many things. Joy, and something else, all mixed together. They are almost home now. But it is not the same as before. They left as one whole group, long ago. They come back with one friend gone. The home below is the same. But they are not the same. Three years changed them all. The long way back is almost done. Something is different now. And Ako-chan feels it, deep inside.
