The friends climb into the flying boat. The white land sits behind them now. The cold cave is gone from sight. Ako-chan looks at the pilot's seat. Obia always flew the boat before. Now that seat is hers. She sits down and holds the controls. Her hands are small on them. But she is ready.

Ako-chan starts the engines. They hum low, then grow strong. The flying boat lifts off the ice. It rises into the pale sky. Slowly, it turns to the north. Below them, the white land falls away. The ice grows smaller and smaller. Soon it is only a thin white line. Then the sea spreads out, grey and wide. Ako-chan holds the boat steady. She points its nose towards home.

The others settle into their places. Dango lies close at her feet. Suke-san sits just behind her, on the right. Daru and Gabu are quiet in their Pods. Ruka rests in her small Pod too. Daru is calm for once, here in the air. Gabu watches the clouds drift past. No one talks much. Each face is quiet and tired. Some of them look back through the windows. The white land is only a pale line now. Then even that line is gone. The engines hum on, steady in the cold air. Now the long flight begins.

Ako-chan looks ahead at the wide grey sea. England is far away. The boat is quiet without Obia's voice. She thinks of the friends behind her. They are all here, safe and warm. She holds the controls a little tighter. To the north. To home. The long way back begins now.
