The cave is quiet now. A pale, cold light comes from outside. It falls on the old ice and the silent machine. The friends are awake. But no one says much. Dango lies close to Ako-chan's feet. Suke-san sits near the icy wall. The tortoises are quiet inside their Pods. The long night is over at last.

Ako-chan stands up slowly. She wants to start the day. She turns to her side, ready to speak. Beside her is an empty space. Obia always stood there. The words are already in her mouth. But no one is there now. There is no blue glow. There is no quiet voice. Ako-chan stops. She closes her mouth. She remembers now. Obia is gone.

The cave feels different today. Something is missing from the air. The cold seems deeper than before. The silence seems wider. The old machine sits dark and still. Its low hum is gone now. No blue glow runs on the icy walls. Dango lifts his head and looks at her. Then he looks at the empty space too. Suke-san's ears turn, but he hears nothing new. Ruka pulls slowly into her shell. No one fills the quiet. The friends only wait in the cold.

Ako-chan reaches into her coat. The red collar is there, against her heart. She holds it for a moment, without a word. Then she lets it rest again. The long journey is over now. They saved the three voices. They are all here, safe in the cold. The whole world feels calm now. But the cave is not full. Someone is missing. That spot stays empty beside her. And Ako-chan can still feel it there.
