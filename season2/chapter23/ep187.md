The friends come out of the cave. The white land is bright and wide. The cold wind blows across the ice. The flying boat waits, ready to go. The pale sky is clear above them. It is time to head north. Pen-san stands close to Ako-chan. He is big now, a tall Emperor. His head rises high above Dango.

Pen-san leans his warm body against her. Ako-chan puts her arms around him. His feathers are smooth under her hands. He is warm against the cold wind. For a moment, neither one moves. Then Pen-san looks down at her. "You will come back?" he asks. "Like last time?" His big dark eyes are full of hope. Ako-chan holds him a little tighter. "I will try," she says.

The wind blows over the white land. Dango looks up at the tall penguin. He still cannot believe how big Pen-san is. Suke-san stands quiet at Ako-chan's side. The tortoises watch from their Pods. Pen-san will stay here, in the south. This wide, white land is his home. But this goodbye is softer than the last one. Obia went far away and will not return. Pen-san is not gone. He is only here, in the snow. One day, Ako-chan can come back to him.

Ako-chan steps back and looks at him. Once, Pen-san was a small gray chick. He could barely walk on the ice. He always wanted her to pick him up. Now he is a fine, strong Emperor. He grew up, just like he said. Ako-chan smiles, even with tears in her eyes. "Goodbye, Pen-san," she says softly. He will be here when she comes back.
