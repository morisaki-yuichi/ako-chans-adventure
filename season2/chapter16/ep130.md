The friends stand together on the ice. The white land is wide and still. Then Ako-chan sees something far off. A tall shape stands on the snow. It is black on top and white below. It starts to move toward them. It comes fast across the ice.

The shape grows bigger as it comes. It is a penguin — a big one. Great black feet thud on the snow. Two large black eyes shine bright. It is Pen-san. But not the small chick from before. He is grown now, big and tall. He runs the last few steps to her. "Ako-chan! I grew up! Just like I said!"

Pen-san pushes his head into her arms. He is so large now, she nearly falls. But his heart is the same as before. He is still her sweet, soft Pen-san. Dango stares up at the huge bird. Once he looked down at a small chick. Now he must look up, far up. His ears fall back in surprise. He does not know what to make of him.

Ako-chan laughs and holds him close. "Pen-san, look at you," she says softly. Her eyes are warm and a little wet. Three years made him an emperor. The small gray chick is gone now. Suke-san watches the tall bird, calm. Even Dango does not bark or fuss. The friends gather close on the ice.

Pen-san leans his head on her shoulder. His big eyes shine with simple joy. He waited for her on the ice. And now, at last, she came back. The chick became a fine young emperor. Just like he always said he would.
