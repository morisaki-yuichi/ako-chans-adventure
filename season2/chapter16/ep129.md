The flying boat comes down low now. Below them spreads the white land. It reaches out in every way. It is flat and wide and pale. There is no sea here, only ice. The great white land lies still. They reach the south at last.

The boat sinks down towards the ground. Ako-chan grips the edge of her seat. The runners touch the icy floor. The whole boat shakes and slides a bit. The ice creaks loud beneath them. Then the boat stops at last. Ako-chan lets out a long breath. They are down on the ground now.

Ako-chan opens the cabin door. Frozen air rushes in around her. It bites at her face and hands. But her warm coat holds it back. She steps out onto the ice. It creaks under her boots. The land runs flat to the far edge. The sky is wide and clear above.

Dango jumps down beside her feet. His boots crunch on the hard snow. Suke-san stands at her right, still. Behind them, the Pods roll to the door. Daru, Gabu, and Ruka watch from inside. The chill wind hums low across the snow. White stretches on as far as she sees. Nothing moves on the great plain.

The land is silent all around. No bird calls. No water moves. Only the wind speaks on the ice. Ako-chan breathes the sharp, clean air. Her breath drifts off in a pale cloud. The chill reaches deep into her chest.

Ako-chan stands quiet on the ice. Three years fall away in a breath. The same cold. The same white world. She knows this place in her heart. I am back, she thinks. We came back at last. The wind carries her words away.
