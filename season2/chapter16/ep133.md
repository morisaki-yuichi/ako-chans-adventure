They step into the dark cave mouth. The light of day falls away behind. Inside, the air is cold and still. Old ice walls rise on every side. The floor is hard and smooth and dark. The way leads down into the hill. No one came here before them.

Obia walks to the front of them. Its blue eyes glow in the dark. The light falls softly on the ice walls. Ako-chan stays close behind the glow. She leads the friends along the path. Their footsteps echo off the cold walls. The sound comes back from far ahead. Step by step, they go deeper in.

The walls shine pale in Obia's light. Old ice holds bubbles deep inside. Some are blue. Some are nearly black. The cave grows colder as they go. Their breath hangs white in the still air. The only light is Obia's blue glow. Beyond it lies a wall of dark.

Down they go, deeper and deeper. The cave is old beyond all years. The stone here never feels the sun. Time seems to stand still down here. Ako-chan keeps her eyes on the light. She does not look back even once.

The signal grows stronger down here. Ako-chan feels it pull her on. It hums in the ice all around. Dango walks close against her leg. Suke-san keeps low and quiet at her right. Even Dalu is still in his Pod. No one speaks in the deep cold.

The path bends down into the dark. The blue light slides along the ice. Ako-chan cannot see the end. But she feels it now, deep and sure. The dark runs far down below them. Something old. Something called them here.
