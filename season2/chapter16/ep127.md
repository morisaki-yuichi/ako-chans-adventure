The cabin grows colder now. Ako-chan can see her own breath. It rises in small white clouds. The window glass is cold to touch. Outside, the sea is dark and icy. Obia stands up by the controls. "The cold is near now," it says.

Obia opens a long metal box. Inside lies the gear they will need. It lifts out a warm white coat. "This is for you, Ako-chan," it says. "It will keep you warm in the ice." The coat is light, but very warm. Ako-chan pulls it on with a smile. Then Obia takes out boots and vests. These are for Dango and Suke-san.

Suke-san steps into his boots at once. He stands calm and ready in his vest. But Dango does not like the boots. He pulls his paw back fast. He shakes one boot off onto the floor. Then he sits down hard and will not move. Suke-san turns and looks at him. He says nothing at all. He only stares, still and sharp. Dango lets out a long, low sigh.

Ako-chan laughs softly at the sight. Slowly, Dango lets the boots go on. He walks a few stiff steps in them. His feet lift high and strange and slow. Soon he forgets them and lies down. The breath of all of them shows white. The cold seeps in along the floor.

Now they all stand ready for the cold. Daru, Gabu, and Ruka stay warm in their Pods. Ako-chan looks out at the dark sea. The white land is close now. She can feel the chill through the glass. Soon they will walk on the ice again.
