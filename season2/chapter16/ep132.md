The next day, they walk the ice. They look for the source of the signal. It calls them on across the snow. Soon a great ice cliff rises ahead. It stands tall and blue and cold. The wind blows hard against its face. They walk along the foot of it.

Pen-san leads them with his big steps. The General marches at his side. Then Gabu stops still in his Pod. His quiet eyes fix on the cliff. "Look. There," he says, low and clear. Everyone turns to where he looks. In the shadow of the ice — a gap. A dark crack opens in the cliff.

They come close to the deep gap. It is a hole in the great wall. Cold air flows out from inside. The opening is tall and black. No one knew this place before. Three years ago, they never saw it. Ako-chan stares into the dark mouth. Her heart beats hard in her chest.

The cliff rises high above them all. Its ice is old and blue. Cracks run through it like dark veins. The wind moans along the frozen wall. Small bits of frost fall now and then. The whole place feels strange and still.

The friends gather at the cave mouth. Dango sniffs the air and growls low. Suke-san's fur stands up on his back. Daru rolls forward for a better look. Ruka stays still and quiet behind. Obia steps up beside Ako-chan now. Its blue eyes turn towards the cave.

Obia is very still for a moment. "The signal," it says. "It comes from inside." A faint pull drifts out of the dark. Ako-chan feels it deep within her. Something waits far back in the ice.
