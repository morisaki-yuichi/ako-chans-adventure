The next day, the sea looks different. The water is dark and very cold. Thin ice floats here and there below. Ako-chan leans close to the window. The light outside is pale and clear. Then she sees it. Something white. Far off on the grey sea.

A great white shape floats on the water. It is the first iceberg. It rises tall and bright from the sea. Its sides shine pale blue in the light. Then another comes. And another after that. Ako-chan's heart beats fast and full. She knows this place. She saw it three years ago. The memory rises up inside her now.

More icebergs lift from the water now. Some are small. Some are tall as hills. They drift in a slow, silent line. The sea between them is deep and black. The chill air seeps into the warm cabin. Ako-chan remembers the cold from before. The wind. The snow. Pen-san's small grey face. It all comes back to her now.

The friends gather close at the window. Dalu rolls his Pod up near the glass. He stares out at the floating ice. "I remember this," he says, soft and low. Gabu comes slowly to his side. He looks for a long, quiet moment. "...Yes," he says. Just that. Dango presses his nose to the glass. Suke-san watches, still and sharp.

Luca looks out from her yellow Pod. The ice drifts past, white and still. The cold comes through the window glass. No one speaks for a long while. Her breath fogs the glass, then fades. The white land of before is near again. Ako-chan watches it come, slow and sure.
