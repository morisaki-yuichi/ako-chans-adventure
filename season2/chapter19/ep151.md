The cave is quiet and cold. The old machine begins to hum again. Ako-chan knows this sound now. It is time for the second jump. She stands close beside Obia. Her friends wait all around her.

The white light wakes and spreads. It sweeps across the waiting friends. This is the second time now. They all know what comes next. The glow moves slowly, one by one. Who will the machine choose this time?

Then the light stops and holds. It falls on Pen-san first. A soft glow wraps his tall body. Next it finds the General at his side. The two penguins shine in the light. The machine makes its choice. Ako-chan and Obia must go on every jump. So now the four stand ready. Pen-san draws a deep, slow breath.

Pen-san shifts on his big feet. His dark eyes are wide and unsure. "Is it my turn now?" he asks. The General stands straight and calm. "Steady," he says. "Stay close to me." His voice is firm and sure. Hard times do not shake him.

Dango throws his weight against Ako-chan's leg. Suke-san watches with sharp, still eyes. Dalu and Gabu wait in their Pods. Luca does not move at all. They will stay here in the cave. The cold glow does not reach them. Pen-san looks back at his friends once. He will go where the others cannot.

Ako-chan rests a hand on Pen-san. "We go together," she says. Her voice is soft and calm. The hum climbs higher in the cold. The light grows bright around the four. The cave begins to fade from sight. Where will it take them this time? No one knows yet.
