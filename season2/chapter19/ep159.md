The cave is quiet and cold. The four rest near the old machine. Pen-san lies still on the ice. His breath is slow but calm now. The friends gather close around them. No one speaks for a long time. The cold air hangs still and heavy.

Everyone feels it now. The jumps grow harder every time. The first jump was hard. The second was harder still. Each one takes more from them. Ako-chan's body still aches deep inside. The next jump will be worse. They all know this now.

Ako-chan looks around at her friends. Pen-san is worn down and weak. Even the strong General looks tired. The jumps take a toll on them all. Each one leaves them more spent. The cave feels colder than before.

Suke-san looks up at the General. His sharp eyes are full of worry. The General looks back at him. He gives a slow, heavy nod. Neither of them says a word. But they both feel the same fear. The jumps are growing too strong.

The old machine hums low and deep. Its light glows softly on the ice. Dango leans close against Ako-chan. Ruka stays small and still nearby. Daru and Gabu wait in their Pods. Worry sits on every quiet face. The whole cave feels heavy and tense.

Ako-chan looks at the old machine. Two voices are safe now. But one more voice still waits. One more jump lies ahead of them. She feels its weight already. One more. But that one will be the hardest. She is not sure they are ready. And maybe it is the most dangerous of all.
