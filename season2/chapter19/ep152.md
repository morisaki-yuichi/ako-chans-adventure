The white light swells and grows. It fills the whole cave at once. It is brighter than the first time. Ako-chan cannot see her hands. The machine roars loudly in her ears. Pen-san presses close to her side.

Then the pull comes, hard and fast. It grabs her whole body at once. But this time it is stronger. It drags the four through the light. Pen-san cries out, loud and sharp. The General holds him close. Ako-chan's head spins, fast and wild. Bright streaks rush past her face. The pull squeezes harder than before. For one long moment, all is light and noise.

Then, all at once, it stops. The roar fades into a low hush. The bright glare drains away to grey. Ako-chan feels new ground beneath her. The air is cold and damp now. A sharp wind bites at her face. It smells of salt and sea. Far off, waves crash and boom. The cold sinks deep into her.

Pen-san shakes and blinks his eyes. The hard jump left him dizzy and weak. The General stands close and steady. "Are you hurt?" he asks. Pen-san shakes his head slowly. He sways, but he stands. The General steadies him with a wing. Obia waits near, its blue eyes calm. The four are still together. This jump is harder than the last.

Ako-chan takes one slow, deep breath. The salt wind fills her chest. Her head still turns from the jump. She opens her eyes at last. A grey sea rolls far below. The sky above is dark with storm. Grey waves swell under a heavy sky. She does not know this place at all.
