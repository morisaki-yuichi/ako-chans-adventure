Then, from the dark behind Pen-san, a sound.

Footsteps. Careful. Even. Each step placed the same distance as the last.

A figure came out of the dark.

Upright. Small but not small in the way that makes you underestimate it. Dark on the back, white on the front. Each eye sharp and calm.

Ako-chan stood.

"Colonel——" she started.

Pen-san, still at her side, said something very quietly.

"General now."

Ako-chan looked at Pen-san. Then at the figure.

General Adelie stopped two steps away. He looked at Ako-chan. Then at Dango. Then, slowly, at each of the others in turn. The pods. Riro's capsule. Suke-san. Obia.

When he reached Obia, he paused. Then he looked back at Ako-chan.

"You came back," he said.

"Yes," she said.

"All of them."

"All of them."

He stood there for a moment. Very straight. Very quiet.

Then he looked at the yellow pod — and inside, Ruka, watching through the wall. He studied her for a moment. Then the green and orange pods. Then Suke-san and Dango.

"Good," he said. Just that.

He walked forward — past Ako-chan, past the group — to Obia.

He stopped in front of Obia. He looked at it for a long time. Obia's eyes were steady. Neither spoke.

Then General Adelie turned and walked back. He stood beside Pen-san.

He was very straight. Very still.

"Follow me," he said. "There is something you need to see."
