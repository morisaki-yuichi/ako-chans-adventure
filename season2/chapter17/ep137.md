The light moves through Obia's body. Its blue eyes flicker on the ice. The friends stand close and watch it. Ako-chan does not say a word. The memory does not come all at once. It returns in small, broken pieces.

Obia goes still, then stiff again. Pictures rise behind its blue eyes. They come and go like quick dreams. Animals. Many animals, in many lands. Voices, soft and far and faint. A sense of something it must do. But the pieces will not join up. Each one slips away before the next.

More pictures flash and then are gone. Wide lands. Far skies. Open seas. Creatures of every shape and kind. Then a hush, and the pictures stop. Obia's body goes tense and cold. The blue light dims, then flares once more.

Obia tries to speak, but cannot. A word starts, then breaks apart. "...so many...," it says, low. Then only the deep hum remains. The thoughts run faster than its voice. Obia cannot hold them still yet. The cave waits for the next word.

Ako-chan watches with a tight chest. She wants to help, but she cannot. Dango whines low down at her feet. Suke-san's ears twitch at every sound. Pen-san presses close to the General. Ruka stays very still in her Pod. No one dares to break the hush. The cave feels colder than before.

Piece by piece, the memory returns. The shape of it grows in the cold. Obia begins to know what it is for. Not all of it. Not yet. But something true takes shape now. A purpose, rising from the mist. Soon, the whole of it will come.
