The marks still glow upon the machine. No one knows where it will send them. The hall is dark and very still. The friends get ready for the jump. Ako-chan stands close beside Obia. Her heart beats hard within her chest.

They make ready now, one by one. Obia checks the glowing panel twice. Ako-chan pulls her warm coat tight. Fear sits heavy in her chest. But she knows that she must go. The dying voices need her now. She will listen for every one. She looks up at Obia and speaks. "I am ready," she says, soft and sure.

Suke-san moves up close to her side. His ears are sharp, his eyes alert. He stands ready, as he always does. The General lifts his head up high. "Stand steady," he says to them all.

Ako-chan thinks of the long road here. The signal. The journey. The friends. All of it brought her to this place. She will not let the voices fade. Her hands are cold, but they hold steady.

The cold air bites at every face. Dango will not leave Ako-chan's feet. He leans his whole weight against her shins. Luca sits small and still and brave in her Pod. The machine hums louder than before. Its light grows bright across the ice. The whole hall seems to lean and wait.

No one knows what waits out there. Fear and courage stand side by side. Ako-chan holds Obia's steady gaze. She does not know. But she will not run. She breathes in deep and holds her ground. The hum climbs higher in the cold. The jump is almost here at last.
