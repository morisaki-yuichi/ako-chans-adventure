Obia stands before the half-buried machine. The others wait behind, still and quiet. Ako-chan holds her breath and watches close. The blue light trembles on the ice. No one says a word in the dark. Obia lifts one silver paw up slowly. It reaches towards the cold, dark metal.

Obia's paw touches the old machine. For a moment, nothing happens at all. Then light runs along the metal side. It glows and spreads beneath the ice. The hum grows low and strong and loud. Obia goes stiff where it stands. Its blue eyes flash and flicker fast. Inside Obia, something starts to move. The locked memory begins to wake.

Obia's voice comes slow and strange. "It is opening," it says. "The memory... is opening." Its silver body shakes with the light. The words come hard, as if from far away. Ako-chan never heard it sound like this before. She wants to reach for Obia. But she does not dare to touch.

The friends watch in frozen silence. Dango presses low against the ground, his ears flat back. Suke-san stands stiff, his eyes hard on the waking metal. A growl sits unspoken in his chest. The glow from the machine still grows. It fills the cold hall with blue. Shadows dance along the ice walls. The whole cave seems to hold its breath.

Obia could not open this memory before. Now it stirs deep in its own body. The light pulses through its hard shell. Something forgotten starts to rise. The thing inside is waking up now. It is coming back to Obia at last. And no one knows what it will be.
