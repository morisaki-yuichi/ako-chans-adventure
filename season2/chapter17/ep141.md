The machine's hum climbs higher now. Its light fills the frozen hall. The air itself begins to shake. The friends stand close in a tight ring. Ako-chan waits at Obia's side. No one moves. No one speaks at all.

The marks race faster on the panel. They blink and shift, bright then dark. The deep hum rises, low and strong. The whole cave trembles with the sound. The old ice walls shiver in the glow. Something huge is waking in the machine.

The air grows thick and hard to breathe. Ako-chan feels the floor throb beneath her. The sound presses in from every side. The old walls groan against the noise. Her whole body shakes with the hum. The cave is full of rising power.

The machine starts to make its choice. It searches through the waiting friends. Who will it pick for the first jump? No one knows. No one can tell. The blue light sweeps across each face. Each one holds their breath and waits. The choice could fall on any of them.

Dango presses hard against her leg. He is shaking now. His ears lie flat, his eyes shut tight. Suke-san stands stiff, his eyes wide on the swelling light. He does not look away from it. Not one of them dares to speak. The cold glare grows too bright to bear.

The hum is now a roar of sound. The light swells white across the ice. Ako-chan shuts her eyes and waits. The first jump is about to begin. There is no turning back now. This is the moment, here and now. The light is everywhere, white and wide. Ako-chan holds on, and waits.
