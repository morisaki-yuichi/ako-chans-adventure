They go on, deep into the cave. The walls press close on either side. Old ice climbs high above their heads. Obia's blue light leads the way. Behind it, the shadows swallow all. The air turns colder with each step they take.

Ako-chan walks close behind the glow. Her breath hangs white before her face. The floor slopes downward beneath their feet. Somewhere ahead, the signal hums on. It is stronger here than before. The hum runs through the ice like a pulse. She stops a moment and listens hard. "Something is down here," she says. "I feel it." Her low voice echoes off the walls.

The friends follow in a quiet line. Dango stays close against her leg. Suke-san moves soft at her right. Pen-san's big feet pad on the floor. The General marches close behind him. Even Daru is still in his Pod. No one speaks in the deep cold.

Far away, the old ice creaks once. The sound rolls down the long tunnel. Then it fades into the black again. Ruka rests quiet in her small Pod. Gabu watches the pale walls slide past. The blue glow slips along the frost.

The walls hold strange shapes within. Frozen bubbles shine in Obia's light. Some glow blue. Some hide in shadow. The path grows narrow, then wide again. A faint draft drifts up from below. It carries the smell of old stone. Ako-chan pulls her warm coat close.

The path bends down and down again. The hum grows louder as they go. Ako-chan keeps her eyes on the light. Something waits at the end of the dark. And it is close now. Very close.
