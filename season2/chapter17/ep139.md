Obia turns to face the machine. Its light is steady now and sure. The friends gather in a close ring. They wait to hear what Obia knows. Ako-chan stands at the very front. It hums low there in its ice.

Obia begins to explain the machine. "It can carry us through time," it says. The places sit ready inside it. But no one knows where they will go. Each place stays hidden till they arrive. Ako-chan and Obia must go each time. The others — the machine will choose them.

"The machine chooses. We do not," Obia says. A panel glows soft upon its side. Strange marks light up, one by one. Some shine, then fade, then glow once more. No one can read what they all mean. The friends watch the lights in silence.

Ako-chan takes in all the words. They will leap through time itself. They cannot pick where they will land. They cannot pick who will come along. The machine holds all of that now. It will decide for them, alone.

Daru rolls closer for a better look. Gabu studies the marks with quiet care. Suke-san stands tense and very still. Pen-san leans near the General again. Ruka does not stir inside her Pod. The cold hum fills the frozen hall. No one says a single word.

She does not know what waits ahead. She does not know who it will choose. They can only wait for it to decide. Ako-chan takes a slow, deep breath. Her face is calm. Her mind is set. The lights blink on across the dark. The journey through time will soon begin.
