The flying boat comes down through soft grey clouds. Below is a small green garden. Ako-chan knows it well. It is her own garden, her own home. She left from here three years ago. Now, at last, she is back. The boat lands gently on the grass. The long journey is finally over.

Ako-chan opens the door of the boat. The autumn air is cool and damp. The light is soft and golden. Yellow leaves lie on the wet grass. The old shed stands quiet in its corner. The house waits behind it, warm and calm. Everything is just as she remembers. Nothing here changed while she was gone. Her heart fills slowly, deep and full.

Dango jumps down onto the grass first. He runs to every corner of the garden. He sniffs the shed, the wall, the door. His whole hind end wags with his tail. He looks back at Ako-chan, bright and glad. Then Ruka's small Pod rolls down too. It does not stop, and it does not wait. It moves straight towards the house. Ruka knows just where to go. Her Pod climbs to the old shelf by the door. That spot was always hers. She settles in, calm and sure.

Ako-chan watches Ruka and smiles. For Ruka, this is home again. The little turtle remembers it all. She did not forget her first home. Ako-chan steps down onto the soft grass. She breathes the cool autumn air. The sky is grey, but the garden feels warm. She is home. The long road is done at last. She stands still for a moment, quiet and full. The garden is calm around her now.
