The days grow shorter now. Autumn slips slowly into winter. The garden light turns thin and cold. Frost shines on the grass each morning. Inside the house, it is warm and calm. The friends settle into their new life. Each one finds a place of their own.

Dalu loves the warm sunny spot. He suns his shell by the bright wall. Gabu likes the cool shade better. He rests under the old garden tree. Suke-san stays just behind Ako-chan. He keeps to her right, calm and watchful. Luca sits high up on her shelf. She watches the whole garden from there. And Dango lies in the very middle. He is happy when they are all near.

Each quiet day holds the same gentle calm. The friends know their corners well now. They move around the garden with ease. The new home does not feel new now. It feels like theirs, warm and safe.

The garden is quiet and full now. Small sounds drift through the cold air. Birds call softly from the bare trees. The kettle hums inside the warm house. Each animal has its own corner now. But they stay close, all the same. A new family is taking shape here. Slowly, it grows warm and whole. Bit by bit, they belong here now.

Ako-chan looks at them all and smiles. Her home is full of quiet life. Dalu, Gabu, Suke-san, Luca, Dango. They are warm, and safe, and near. It is a calm and peaceful evening. The lamp glows softly in the window. Outside, the winter night is still. But the family is not quite whole. One friend is still not here.
