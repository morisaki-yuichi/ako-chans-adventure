Winter comes fully to the garden now. The light is thin and pale and grey. The old shed stands silent in its corner. Beside it is a bare patch of ground. Nothing sits there. No one stands there. The cold grass grows flat and low. The spot waits, but it stays empty.

That ground was Obia's place. Obia used to stand by the old shed. At night, it watched over the quiet garden. It stayed close beside Ako-chan, always near. Now the shed stands alone in the cold. The long night watch is over. No blue light glows in the dark. Obia's old corner is empty now.

On quiet nights, Ako-chan still looks for it. She turns towards the shed in the dark. For a moment, she waits for the blue glow. But the corner stays dark and still. Then she remembers. Obia is gone now.

The other animals feel the change too. Dango walks wide around that ground. He does not lie on that bare grass. Suke-san passes by it without a sound. Even Dalu's quick Pod slows down there. The garden holds the shape of someone gone. That lonely place stays still and bare. No one fills that corner now.

Ako-chan stands at the window and looks out. Her eyes rest on that one corner. She remembers Obia in the dark garden. The pale blue eyes. The quiet, steady watch. The long journey is over now. They all came home at last. The garden is full of life again. But Obia did not come back. That one place stays empty in the cold. She lets the stillness settle around her.
