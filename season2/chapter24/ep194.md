For Dalu, Gabu, and Suke-san, this place is new. They never saw England before today. The garden is wide and green and wet. Strange trees stand along the stone wall. The light here is soft and pale. It is the gentle light of the north. Everything looks and smells unfamiliar. But it is quiet here, and gentle.

Dalu does not wait at all. He walks fast inside his red Pod. The Pod spins and races across the grass. He wants to see every corner at once. Round and round he goes, quick and bright. He is glad to run free again. Gabu is different from Dalu. His blue Pod moves, slow and careful. He stops, he looks, and he thinks. Then he moves a little farther. He checks each new thing before he trusts it.

Suke-san walks low across the wet grass. He reads the ground with his sharp nose. Every smell is new and strange to him. Rain, wet leaves, cold stone, far houses. He learns the whole garden through his nose. His ears stay up, alert and still. Nothing escapes his careful nose. Dango trots close beside his old friend. He shows Suke-san the best corners of the garden.

Ako-chan watches her three friends explore. Each one finds the place in their own way. Dalu runs, Gabu studies, Suke-san smells. The garden is strange to them right now. But soon it will not feel strange. They will learn its corners and its smells. This green, wet garden will hold them all. It will be their new home together. Step by step, it becomes their own. Ako-chan knows they will be happy here.
