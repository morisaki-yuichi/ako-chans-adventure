The next morning, the garden is calm and bright. Autumn sun falls on the cold grass. Dalu, Gabu, and Suke-san still feel lost. Where is the warm spot? Where is it safe? They look around, unsure and slow. This place is new to all three of them. They do not know where to go. The garden keeps all its secrets from them.

But Luca knows. Luca knows the way. She lived here before the long journey. She remembers every corner of this garden. Her small Pod rolls out across the grass. She moves slowly, but she does not stop. She is going somewhere, and she knows where. She does not even pause to think. The three friends watch her and follow. They cannot guess how she remembers it.

Luca leads them to the sunny wall. The morning light pools warm on the stone. It is the best warm spot in the garden. Dalu, Gabu, and Suke-san gather there. The smallest one shows them the way. Then Luca moves on to a quiet corner. It is safe there, out of the cold wind. The three friends follow her once more. She knows this place best of all. Every warm stone, every patch of sun.

Ako-chan stands by the door and watches. She sees Luca lead them on. The newest, smallest friend goes first now. The bigger ones follow close behind her. Luca does not look back, not once. She knows each turn of the garden. The three friends stay near her small Pod. It is a strange and gentle sight. This morning, the garden feels like home. Ako-chan watches by the door, warm and quiet.
