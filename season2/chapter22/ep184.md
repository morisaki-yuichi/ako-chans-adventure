Then the machine wakes. A deep sound rises from inside it. The light at the door grows bright. It fills the cold, dark cave. Everyone goes still. The time is here now. Ako-chan's heart beats hard and fast.

Obia walks towards the machine. Then it stops. It turns back to Ako-chan. Slowly, it takes off its red collar. It holds the collar out to her. "Keep this," it says. "I do not know why. But it is important." Ako-chan takes the collar in her hands. It is warm from Obia's body. Her hands shake hard. She cannot find a word. Tears stand in her eyes. She holds the collar against her heart. Obia turns away and steps towards the light.

The deep sound grows louder and louder. The light burns brightly around Obia. It is too bright to look at now. The friends press close behind Ako-chan. Dango whines low in his throat. Suke-san stands stiff and still. Pen-san goes very quiet beside him. The General holds a long, deep salute. Daru, Gabu, and Ruka watch from the floor. Their small heads turn up to Obia. The whole cave shines blue and white. Ako-chan holds the warm collar tight. She reaches her other hand towards Obia. But she cannot move her feet. She wants to run to it. But she cannot.

Obia stops inside the light. It turns back one last time. It looks at them all, slow and calm. Its blue eyes shine, soft and warm. "I will not forget you," it says. Then the light swells and spreads. It fills the whole cave. It covers Obia. It covers everything.
