The machine is quiet for now. But the goodbye begins. The friends gather close around Obia. The cave is full of soft, deep silence. Obia's blue eyes glow in the dark. Everyone knows the time is near.

One by one, they come to Obia. Pen-san leans down and presses his cheek close. His big body trembles just a little. The General gives a slow, deep salute. Then the three tortoises move. Daru climbs out of his red Pod. Gabu steps down from his blue Pod. Even Ruka leaves her yellow Pod. They come near to Obia, slow and careful. Their small shells touch Obia's cold feet. Dango pushes his head against Obia's side. Each one says goodbye in its own quiet way.

Then it is Ako-chan's turn. She kneels down beside Obia. Her hands shake in the cold air. She wants to say so many things. But the words will not come out. Her heart is too full and too sad. The deep silence fills the whole cave. The blue eyes look up at her. Everyone waits, quiet and still. The cold air does not move at all. Dango leans his warm weight against her knee.

At last, Ako-chan finds her voice. "Obia... thank you," she says. "For everything." Her voice is small and soft. It almost breaks in the quiet. The words feel far too small for this. But they are all she has. She blinks back her hot, stinging tears. Obia does not look away. It looks at her for a long, long moment. The light in its eyes is warm and steady. Ako-chan will hold this look forever.
