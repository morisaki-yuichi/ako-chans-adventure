The cave is quiet now. The old machine hums, low and soft. Ako-chan sits on the cold ground. Her friends press close around her. Everyone is back. Everyone is safe. Obia stands by the machine. Its blue eyes glow in the dark. The hard work is almost done.

Obia sends the last voice into the future. The light runs brightly along the metal. Then it settles, slow and calm. Now the three voices are safe. The wolf, from the snowy mountain. The seabird, from the storm island. And the ancient one, from the ice age. Three voices, saved from time. Ako-chan lets out a long, slow breath. She did it. They all did. "We did it," she says. "We saved them." Her voice is soft and tired. Dango pushes his head into her side.

The friends draw in closer. Dalu and Gabu rest in their Pods. Their lights glow softly, red and blue. Luca is still and quiet beside them. Pen-san leans against Ako-chan's arm. The General stands tall and proud. The cave feels warm with them all. Outside, the wind is far away. For one small moment, everything feels calm. Ako-chan closes her eyes and rests. The worst is far behind them now.

But Obia does not rest. It stands very still by the machine. Its blue eyes do not turn to the friends. They look past everyone, far away. Ako-chan opens her eyes and watches it. The machine still hums under its paw. Something is not right. She feels it, deep and cold. She gets to her feet, slow and unsure. The warm calm of the cave fades away.
