The long hours pass in the cold cave. No one sleeps well. The machine still hums and leaks. Morning is hard to feel down here. Ako-chan sits close to Obia. Her friends rest in a quiet ring. The cold seeps deep into her bones. The question hangs in the cold air. Who will go through the door?

Obia turns to Ako-chan. Its blue eyes are calm and still. "It must be me," it says. "I came from there." The future is the place Obia came from. Obia can cross back. The others cannot. Ako-chan's lips tremble. She shakes her head, slow and hard. "No," she wants to say. But no words come out. Her throat is too tight. Her chest aches with the cold. She wants to beg it to stay. Obia watches her, quiet and gentle. It does not look away from her.

The friends are silent around them. Dango's tail does not move at all. Suke-san lowers his head low. Daru and Gabu stay still in their Pods. Even Ruka does not stir. Pen-san looks from Obia to Ako-chan. The General stands like cold, grey stone. The blue light falls on every quiet face. The cave feels colder than ever. No one can find a single word.

Obia is calm. Too calm, Ako-chan thinks. She sees it clearly now. Obia made its choice already. It chose long before this moment. There is no fear in its blue eyes. There is only a quiet, steady light. The choice is made. Nothing can change it now. Ako-chan feels her heart break, slow and quiet. But she will not look away from Obia.
