The light fills the cave. It is bright and white and cold. Then, slowly, it fades. It grows smaller and smaller. Then it is gone. The cave is dark again. And it is very quiet. The blue glow is gone from the walls. The cold dark presses in on them.

Ako-chan opens her eyes. The door of light is closed now. Obia stood here just a moment ago. Now that place is empty. There is nothing there at all. Ako-chan reaches out her hand. It reaches into the empty air. Her fingers close on nothing. Obia is gone. Obia went through to the future. It went to close the door from the far side.

No one moves. No one makes a sound. Dango stares at the empty place. Suke-san stands frozen and still. The tortoises do not stir in the dark. Pen-san and the General do not move. The machine is silent now. The cold does not leak from it anymore. Even the wind outside is far away. No sound comes from anywhere at all. The cave is completely quiet.

Ako-chan's other hand holds the red collar tight. It is still a little warm. Obia went to the future. It will not come back. Not ever again. The long journey saved the three voices. But it took Obia far away. The friends stand close in the cold dark. They are only quiet shadows now. No one knows what to do. Ako-chan stares at the empty air. Her hand hangs there in the dark. She cannot make herself move at all. The empty place stays empty. The cave holds only silence now.
