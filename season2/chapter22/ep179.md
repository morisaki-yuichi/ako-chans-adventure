Obia moves close to the machine. It puts one paw on the cold metal. Its blue eyes flicker fast now. The friends watch and wait. No one speaks. The low hum grows under the ice. The cave feels colder than before. Ako-chan steps closer to Obia.

Obia is quiet for a long moment. It reads something deep in the machine. Then it understands. The machine is still open. It made a door between now and the future. And the door did not close. Obia turns to Ako-chan. "The door is open," it says. "The future is leaking through." A thin, cold air seeps from the machine. It is not the cold of the cave. It comes from somewhere far away. It comes from the other side.

Ako-chan holds her breath. Dango presses against her leg. Suke-san steps back, low and tense. His fur stands up along his back. Pen-san goes very still. The General does not move at all. The cold air touches Ako-chan's face. It feels wrong. Something far away does not belong here. Daru and Gabu draw back in their Pods. Even Ruka is afraid. The blue light shakes across the cold walls. The whole cave feels thin and strange. They saved the voices. But it was not enough.

Ako-chan looks at the open machine. The thin cold keeps seeping out. Far away, something is breaking. And it is leaking into now. The future pours through the door. The door is open. They have to close it. Ako-chan stares at the cold, blue light. A slow fear rises in her chest. She does not look away from it.
