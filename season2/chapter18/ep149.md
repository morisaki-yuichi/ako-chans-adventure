The wolf's voice still rings in Ako-chan. But it is time to go home. Obia lifts its head and speaks. "Hold on," it says. "We jump back now." The light begins to swell again. It grows bright around the four. Ako-chan takes a deep, steady breath.

The light flares white and harsh. Then something pulls them hard once more. This time the pull is stronger. It grips her whole body and squeezes. Her ears ring. Her head spins fast. The snow and the wolves fall away. For one long moment, all is light. The jump back is harder than before.

Then, all at once, it stops. The bright glare drains away again. The cold air of the cave returns. Hard ice lies beneath her feet. The old machine hums close by. Its light glows softly on the walls. They are back in the deep cave.

Ako-chan blinks in the dim cave light. The world still turns around her. Slowly, it settles and grows still. The cold ice presses up through her boots. The deep hum of the machine fills the air. She is home, but her head still swims.

The friends run to them at once. Dalu and Gabu roll fast in their Pods. Pen-san hurries close on his big feet. Luca moves slowly behind them all. The General stands tall and watches. They waited here the whole time. Now they see the four are safe.

Ako-chan stands still among her friends. Her body aches from the hard jump. But her mind is far away. She still hears the old wolf's voice. Its proud words echo deep inside. It will not leave her, even now.
