The white light fills the frozen hall. It sweeps across the waiting friends. Ako-chan stands very still beside Obia. The machine hums low and deep. Its glow moves over them, one by one. Who will it choose? No one knows yet.

Then the light stops. It falls on Dango first. A soft blue glow wraps around him. Next it finds Suke-san at her side. The two of them shine in the cold light. The machine makes its choice at last. Ako-chan and Obia must go on every jump. So now they stand together, ready.

Dalu and Gabu watch from inside their Pods. Luca stays small and still beside them. Their eyes turn towards the four. They will wait here in the deep cave. The glow does not reach them.

Ako-chan looks back at her friends. She does not want to leave them behind. But the machine chose, and they must go. She lifts her hand towards them. "We will come back," she says. Her voice is soft, but it does not shake.

Why did it pick these two? No one knows. The machine gives no answer at all. It chose, and that is all they have. Dango shoulders into her leg. Suke-san stands tall, his ears sharp and high.

Suke-san looks back at the General once. He gives a short, firm nod goodbye. The General lifts his head and nods back. Dango whines low and licks Ako-chan's hand. She rests her palm on his warm head. The four of them are ready now.

The light grows bright around the four. The hum climbs higher in the frozen air. Ako-chan holds steady at Obia's side. The cave and the friends start to fade. There is no turning back now. The jump is here.
