Ako-chan stands in the space between times. It is gray and still all around her. No ground. No sky. Only Obia floats here beside her. She holds tight to its cool, smooth side. She is not alone in the gray. Far off, a thin line of light glows. The three friends left it behind.

Obia follows the thin trail with its eyes. The line twists and bends in the gray. It is faint. It is hard to hold. Ako-chan looks up at the pale blue glow. "Can you follow them?" she asks. Her voice shakes a little. "I will try," says Obia. Its voice is calm and sure. But it cannot promise more than this. The same place. The same time. It does not know if they will be there.

Obia's eyes shine, bright and hard. They lock onto the thin line. Ako-chan closes her own eyes tight. She holds the three friends in her mind. She thinks of Daru's quick feet. She thinks of Gabu, calm and slow. She thinks of small Ruka, lost and afraid. Please, she thinks. Please let us reach them. Let them all be safe. The gray light starts to twist around them. A strong pull grips her whole body. The jump begins. It is rough and wild this time.

The gray light pulls and tears at them. Ako-chan holds on with all her strength. The trail glows ahead, thin and faint. She does not know where they will land. Obia does not know either. They follow the faint trail into the dark. Wherever it leads, they will go too. They will not stop until they find them.
