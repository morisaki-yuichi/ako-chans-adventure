The voice is safe, and the work is done. Now it is time to go home. Ako-chan gathers her friends close to her. Dalu waits near in his red Pod. Gabu waits near in his blue Pod. Little Luca sits close in her yellow Pod. The three small shells are together at last.

Ako-chan stands and looks up high. The giant tortoise looks back down at her. Its old eyes are calm and kind. It is not angry. It never was. Dalu and Gabu look up too, in awe. Ako-chan puts her hand on her heart. "Thank you," she says, soft and clear. "We will not forget." The ancient one dips its great head. It is slow, and deep, and full of peace. Then it grows still once more. It watches them with quiet, old eyes.

Obia comes to the centre of them all. Its blue glow grows bright and warm. "Hold on to me," it says to them. Ako-chan holds Luca's small Pod tight. Dalu and Gabu press in close. Luca stays calm inside her Pod. She is not afraid of the great one now. The five of them stand as one now. The air starts to hum and shake. A soft light rises all around them. It wraps the five friends in its glow. This time, they all go together.

Ako-chan looks up one last time. The great shell watches, calm and still. It will stand here for many more years. "Goodbye," she says, soft and low. Her friends press warm against her. The light grows bright around the five. This time, they will all go home. This time, they go home together.
