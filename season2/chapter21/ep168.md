Dalu opens his eyes. The cave is gone. The snow is gone. His red Pod sits on cold, hard ground. The land is wide and grey. A cold wind blows across it. Strange, huge plants rise all around him. He does not know this place at all.

Gabu's blue Pod rests close by on the grey ground. Dalu turns his head fast. He looks for the others. Then he sees a small yellow shape. It is Luca, in her little Pod. She is safe. Gabu is safe too. But the others are gone. Ako-chan is not here. Obia is not here. They are all alone. Dalu's heart beats fast. "Where... are we?" he says. His thin voice fades in the wind. No one answers him.

The wind is cold and sharp. It pulls at the tall, strange plants. The sky is wide and flat and grey. There is no sun in it. Far off, the land runs to the edge of sight. Nothing moves out there. No bird. No sound. Just the cold wind and the grey. Dalu looks at Luca again. She does not move. She only stares at the hard ground. Deep inside her shell, she shivers — not from the cold, but from fear. The shock holds her still.

Dalu is afraid too. But he does not show it. He rolls his Pod slowly across to Luca's side. He stops right next to her small Pod. "I am here," he says softly. "We are here with you." Gabu's Pod comes close on her other side. The three small Pods press together in the cold. They do not know this place. They do not know the time. And they do not know if anyone will come.
