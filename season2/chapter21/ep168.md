Daru opens his eyes. The cave is gone. The snow is gone. He stands on cold, hard ground. The land is wide and gray. A cold wind blows across it. Strange, huge plants rise all around him. He does not know this place at all.

Gabu stands close by. His blue Pod rests on the gray ground. Daru turns his head fast. He looks for the others. Then he sees a small yellow shape. It is Ruka, in her little Pod. She is safe. Gabu is safe too. But the others are gone. Ako-chan is not here. Obia is not here. They are all alone. Daru's heart beats fast. "Where... are we?" he says. His thin voice fades in the wind. No one answers him.

The wind is cold and sharp. It pulls at the tall, strange plants. The sky is wide and flat and gray. There is no sun in it. Far off, the land runs to the edge of sight. Nothing moves out there. No bird. No sound. Just the cold wind and the gray. Daru looks at Ruka again. She does not move at all. Her small shell trembles in the cold. She only stares at the hard ground. The shock holds her still.

Daru is afraid too. But he does not show it. He walks slowly across to Ruka's side. He stops right next to her small Pod. "I am here," he says softly. "We are here with you." Gabu comes close on her other side. The three of them press together in the cold. They do not know this place. They do not know the time. And they do not know if anyone will come.
