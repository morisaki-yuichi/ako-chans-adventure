Ako-chan runs toward the huge dark shape. It grows bigger with every step. Then she sees it clear at last. It is a tortoise, vast and old. Its shell rises up like a great hill. And there, below it, are three small shapes.

Ako-chan's breath catches in her throat. Daru is there. Gabu is there. And little Ruka is there too. Ruka sits still on the cold ground. She looks up at the giant tortoise. She is not hurt. She is safe. Daru and Gabu stand close beside her. They kept her safe through it all. Ako-chan runs the last few steps. She drops down beside the three friends. "You are safe!" she cries. "You are all safe!" Tears run warm down her cold face.

Daru rushes to her, fast and glad. He bumps his shell against her hand. Gabu comes slowly, calm as always. He stays close by Ako-chan's knee. His careful eyes are soft with relief. High above them, the great shell waits. The old eyes look down, calm and deep. Ruka turns her small head at last. She looks at Ako-chan, then back up high. The huge tortoise does not move at all.

Ako-chan holds them close, all three. She made it. She found them in time. She was so afraid for them. Now the heavy fear melts a little inside her. They are safe. Nothing else matters now. She holds Ruka's small shell with care. She feels the warmth of all three friends. Obia stands quiet at her side. Its calm glow shines on the small shells. For this one quiet moment, they are all together again.
