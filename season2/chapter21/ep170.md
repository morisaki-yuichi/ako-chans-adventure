The three small Pods still wait on the cold ground. Then a shadow falls over them. It is wide and dark. It covers the grey sky. The light goes dim around them. Dalu looks up slowly. Gabu looks up too. Something very big stands above them.

It is a tortoise. But it is huge. Its shell rises up like a hill. Dalu cannot see the top of it. The great shell blocks out the sky. The ancient one moves slowly above them. One huge foot lifts and sets down. The hard ground shakes a little. Dust rises and drifts in the cold air. Then the giant tortoise stops. Its old eyes look down at the three. They are calm and deep and slow. The three small tortoises cannot move.

Dalu stares up at the high wall of shell. He opens his mouth to speak. But no words come out. For once, Dalu has nothing to say. His quick feet do not move at all. Gabu is silent beside him. Gabu's careful eyes stay wide and round. Even the cold wind seems to stop. Time itself feels still and frozen. Luca looks up at the dark form above. Her small body does not tremble now. She is calm under those ancient eyes.

The giant tortoise does not move towards them. It does not make a sound. Dalu waits for fear to come. But the deep eyes hold no anger. It does not want to hurt them. It only looks. It is just watching the three small ones. The three of them stare back up in silence. And far off, no one else comes for them.
