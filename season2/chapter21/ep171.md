Suke-san waits by the cave wall. The ice is cold and grey around him. Dango is here too. Pen-san stands tall and still. The General waits near the old machine. The jump light is gone now. The cave is quiet, dark, and cold.

They saw the five friends off long ago. Ako-chan and Obia. Daru, Gabu, and little Ruka. The light took them, and then they were gone. The first jump came back soon. The second jump came back soon too. But this time is not the same. No light comes. No one returns. The wait grows long. Too long. Suke-san lifts his nose. He feels it deep inside. "Too long," he says. "Something is wrong."

Suke-san looks up the long, dark way they came. Far above, the cave mouth is just a thin grey light. No shape moves in it. No one comes down to them. The way out is a long climb, and the cold snow lies far beyond it. He turns back to the others.

Dango cannot stay still. He walks to the machine. He walks back to Ako-chan's empty place. His feet tap on the cold ice. He whines, low and soft, deep in his chest. Pen-san watches the dark with big eyes. He does not understand it. But he feels the fear all the same. He shifts from one foot to the other. The cave drips, slow and far away. Time moves on, and no one comes.

The General stands straight and still. He looks at the quiet machine. "...We wait," he says. "It is all we can do." No one knows what went wrong. No one knows where the friends are. The four of them wait in the cold. And the empty cave waits with them.
