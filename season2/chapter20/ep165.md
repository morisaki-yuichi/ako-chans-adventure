The crack closes slowly in the storm. The black mouth seals shut. The wild light grows soft and dim. The roar fades into a low hum. Then, at last, it stops. Everything goes quiet and still. The wild storm is over now.

Ako-chan opens her eyes. The storm is gone. The red, blue, and yellow lights are gone. Daru, Gabu, and Ruka are gone too. Only Obia stands beside her. The two of them float in a strange place. The jump did not finish.

This is not the cave. This is not the snow. This is not any time at all. It is the space between times. Not here. Not there. Just gray and still. No ground. No sky. Only a pale, empty light.

Ako-chan looks all around her. There is nothing to see. There is nothing to hear. The pale light has no end. It does not move. It does not change. No up and no down. Nothing is warm. Nothing is cold. Her own breath makes no sound. She feels very small and alone.

Obia floats close to her side. Its pale blue eyes glow softly. Ako-chan reaches for its cool, smooth side. They are the only light in the gray. The three friends are far from this place. They fell through the crack to somewhere else. To some other time, far away.

But where did they go? To when did they fall? Ako-chan does not know. Obia does not know. No one knows at all. The three turtles are lost in time. They are alone in the gray. The two of them, and nothing else. And the empty quiet holds only two.
