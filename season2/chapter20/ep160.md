The cave is cold and still. The third jump waits ahead. Everyone feels its heavy weight. Ako-chan stands near the old machine. Its low hum fills the dark. The pale light moves on the ice. No one steps towards it yet. The air is thick with doubt.

Suke-san steps forward. He stands at Ako-chan's right, a little back. His sharp eyes are full of worry. He saw her grow weak. Each jump takes more from her body. Her hands shook after the last one. The pain runs deeper every time. He cannot stay quiet now. "The jumps are too strong now," he says. "We should stop."

The General stands tall on the ice. The white ring around his eye is bright. He looks at Ako-chan for a long moment. Then he gives a short, firm nod. "I agree," he says. "It is too dangerous." His voice is hard. But his worry is real and deep. He fears for the girl, not the mission.

Ako-chan looks at them both. They are not afraid for themselves. They are afraid for her. Dango tucks his side against her leg. He is quiet, but his eyes are wide. Dalu and Gabu wait in their Pods. Even Dalu does not move. Luca stays small and still nearby. The whole cave waits for her word.

The machine hums low and deep. Its light moves across the cold ice. Two voices are safe now. But one more still waits somewhere in time. No one else can hear it. Ako-chan holds her hands tight. Should they stop here? Should they go on? She does not answer yet. The cave holds its breath.
