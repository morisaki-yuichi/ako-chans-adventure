The crack grows wide in the spinning light. The white tears open like a mouth. Inside, all is black and deep. The storm grips all five. The light pulls and twists without end. Ako-chan holds on as hard as she can. But the pull is far too strong.

The five spin inside the wild storm. Daru and Gabu roll in their Pods. Obia holds close to Ako-chan's side. They all fight to stay together. But the storm throws them every way. No one can hold a steady place.

Ruka is the smallest of them all. Her yellow Pod spins near the crack. She cannot hold on. She cannot fight it. The dark mouth pulls at her Pod. Slowly, then fast, it draws her in. The little light slides toward the black.

"Ruka!" Ako-chan cries out. She reaches out her hand. Her fingers stretch through the wild light. She tries to catch the small Pod. But it is too far away. The space between them grows wider. The pull tears them apart. Ako-chan's hand closes on nothing.

The yellow light slips into the crack. It grows small, then smaller still. For one moment, it shines in the dark. Then the black swallows it whole. Ruka is gone. The little Pod is gone. The light is gone.

Ako-chan stares at the empty dark. Her hand still reaches out. Her mouth is open, but no sound comes. Her eyes burn with hot tears. The storm roars all around her. She cannot hear her own voice. The roar takes it away. One friend is lost. The smallest one of all. And she could not hold on.
