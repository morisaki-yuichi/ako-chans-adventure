Ako-chan sits in the gray light. Her sobs grow slow and quiet. The empty place is cold and still. No sound moves in the gray. Then she feels something near her. Obia stands close beside her. Its pale blue eyes are soft and warm. It does not move away.

Obia does not blame her. It does not say one hard word. It does not speak of the lost jump. It bends down close to her face. Its calm glow shines on her wet cheeks. "We will go together," it says. Its voice is steady and sure. "We will find them."

Ako-chan looks up at the soft blue glow. The words reach deep inside her. She is not alone in this place. Obia is here with her. Slowly, her tears begin to stop. She wipes her face with her hand. A small, brave hope grows in her. Her heart is not so heavy now.

Obia turns to the empty gray. Its eyes search the quiet space. Then it finds a thin line of light. The three friends left this faint trail. It runs off into the deep dark. Obia follows it with its careful eyes. It is faint, but it is real. It is the way to their friends.

"Hold on to me," Obia says. Ako-chan holds its side tight. She will not let go. She takes one slow, deep breath. Her fear grows quiet inside her. The thin trail glows ahead of them. Together, they turn to face it. Then the two of them jump after their friends. They go to find the ones they lost. Wherever they are. Whenever they are.
