The old machine hums louder now. Its light fills the whole cave. The symbols on the panel begin to glow. The machine is choosing again. Everyone holds still and waits. Who will it pick this time?

The light reaches out across the ice. It wraps around Daru's red Pod. It wraps around Gabu's blue Pod. Then it wraps around Ruka's small yellow Pod. Three turtles. And Ako-chan and Obia, as always. Dango, Suke-san, Pen-san, and the General stay back. They watch the five from the dark, quiet and tense. Dango cannot take his wide eyes off Ako-chan. A low whine builds in his throat and will not stop.

"We will come back," Ako-chan says. The light grows bright around them. It pulls at her body, strong and fast. The cold cave fades away. The faces of the four grow dim. White light spins all around them. The jump begins. Ako-chan holds Daru's Pod close. Ruka's small Pod glows near her side. Her heart beats fast and loud.

But the jump is going wrong. The light twists in a strange way. It does not feel like before. The pull turns hard and wrong. The spin grows sharp and wild. A deep crack runs through the white. It splits the spinning light in two. Obia's eyes flash brightly. "Something is wrong!" it says. "Hold on!"

Ako-chan grips tight. The light bends and tears around them. The spinning storm catches all five. Ako-chan cannot see her friends now. Far behind, the four in the cave see only light. They cannot see the crack inside the jump. Deep in the spin, something begins to come apart.
