For one heartbeat, no one moves. The yellow light is gone. The crack still hangs open in the storm. Dalu sees it. Gabu sees it too. Their friend is on the other side. They do not stop to think.

Dalu moves first. He runs hard inside his Pod. His legs push, fast and wild. The red Pod shoots towards the dark crack. "Luca! I'm coming!" he cries. His voice is loud and fierce. The red light dives into the black. He does not look back even once.

Gabu moves next. He says no word at all. But his choice is just as strong. He walks straight and sure inside his Pod. His steps are slow but full of will. The blue Pod rolls towards the crack. Then it tips over the dark edge. The blue light follows the red into the deep.

Red and blue chase the yellow. Three small lights, gone into the black. Ako-chan reaches out for them. "No! Wait!" she cries. But a new pull takes her now. It drags her another way. It tears her and Obia from the crack. Their hands cannot reach the closing dark.

Ako-chan watches the three lights fade. Her heart breaks to see them go. She cannot move towards the crack. The storm keeps her far away. Obia holds tight beside her. It stays close, and does not let go. The storm pulls two of them one way. The other three fall the other way. The dark mouth begins to close. Only she and Obia remain. The storm pulls them somewhere else. The crack is gone. Their three friends are gone too.
