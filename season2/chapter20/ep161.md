Ako-chan is quiet for a long time. The cave waits all around her. Suke-san's words hang in the cold air. The General stands straight and still. Dango looks up at her face. Everyone waits to hear her choice.

She thinks of the wolf in the snow. She thinks of the gentle seabirds. Two voices are safe now. But one more waits, alone, somewhere in time. If they stop now, no one will save it. No one else can hear that voice. Only she can. The thought sits heavy in her chest. She knows what she must do.

Ako-chan lifts her head. Her face is set and calm. She holds one hand tight at her side. "I will go," she says. Her voice does not shake. Her eyes are clear now. "We are so close." She chooses this herself. No one pushes her forward. The choice is hers, and hers alone.

Obia stands close beside her. Its pale blue eyes glow softly. It does not try to stop her. It will go where she goes. Dango sets himself against her leg. His body is warm and close. He does not understand her words. But he feels her choice, and stays. The friends look at one another. Daru, Gabu, and Ruka wait in their red, blue, and yellow Pods.

The old machine hums and glows. Its light grows bright on the ice. Ako-chan looks at the waiting light. She breathes in the cold, sharp air. She is afraid deep inside. But she will not turn back now. Her friends gather close behind her. One more voice. One more jump. She does not know what this choice will cost her.
