A new Pod waits inside the flying boat. Ako-chan carries it out to Daru. It is round and clear, like the old one. But this Pod is brand new and clean. She sets it down on the white rock. "This one is for you, Daru," she says.

Daru looks at the Pod with quick eyes. He walks up to it, slow at first. Then he steps inside the clear ball. He stands still for one short moment. Then his little legs begin to move. They move faster and faster on the floor. The Pod starts to roll across the rock. It rolls fast, then very fast. A red light glows bright all around him. "Fast! It is fast!" Daru says.

The red glow is just the same as before. Daru had a Pod like this once. Back then, these rocks were all new to him. Now he knows each stone by heart. He runs his Pod the way he always did. Full speed, and full of pure joy.

Ako-chan laughs out loud at the sight. Dust and dry sand fly up behind the Pod. Dango runs after it across the rocks. He barks and barks, happy and loud. The red Pod shines hard in the sun. It races over the stone in wide circles. The blue sea flashes past behind it. Obia watches it all from beside the boat. The whole shore rings with Daru's happy noise.

The Pod slows down near Ako-chan's feet. Daru looks up at her, out of breath. His eyes are bright and full of light. Three years are gone now. But Daru is still Daru. Fast, glad, and never still for long.
