Gabu stands on the warm sand now. He came down slow from the high rocks. Ako-chan kneels beside him in the heat. She holds another Pod from the boat. It is round and clear, like Daru's. But this light will not glow red.

"This one is for you, Gabu," she says. Gabu looks at the Pod for a moment. Then he steps inside, slow and sure. He does not rush, the way Daru did. He starts to walk at an even pace. Step, step, step — one steady beat. The Pod begins to roll in a smooth line. A soft blue light glows all around him. It does not race. It moves calm and sure. "...Thank you," Gabu says. Just two small words.

Daru rolls up fast beside him. His red Pod is quick and bright. Gabu's blue Pod is calm and slow. The two move side by side on the sand. Ako-chan saw these two shells travel once. Now they roll along together once again.

The hot wind moves across the open sand. It lifts the dust in low, dry waves. Dango watches the blue Pod roll past. His ears turn to follow the sound. Daru rolls in loud, happy loops nearby. But Gabu holds one calm, even line. Obia stands by the boat and watches it. Gabu keeps his steady beat inside. He is glad to be here again.

Two shells move on the sand again. One races on. One keeps a quiet beat. But they both go the same way now. Ako-chan smiles down at the two friends. They travel south together now. The journey holds them all once more.
