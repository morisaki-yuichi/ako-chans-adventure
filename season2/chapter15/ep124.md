The flying boat lifts off the green hill. It climbs back up into the grey sky. Inside, the cabin is warm and bright. Everyone is here, in their own place. Ako-chan looks around at them all. Her heart is full and glad.

Dango lies close at her feet. Suke-san sits at her right, a step behind. Obia stands quiet by the controls. Daru spins fast in his red Pod. Gabu rolls slow in his blue Pod. And Ruka rests in her small yellow Pod. Seven of them, together at last. Ako-chan counts each dear face in turn. Not one friend is missing now.

Three small shells travel side by side. Red, blue, and yellow, in a row. But the yellow Pod is the smallest one. Ruka is the littlest of the three. She is also the newest friend of all. Daru and Gabu look at the small newcomer. She rests there calm and still, as always. Daru is loud. Gabu is slow. But little Ruka is the quiet one. The three shells are not the same at all.

The boat hums on, deep and steady. Clouds drift past the wide windows. Grey sea spreads far below them all. The cabin is warm against the cold air. Dango lifts his head and looks around. He sniffs each one, slow and glad. Then he lies back down at her feet.

Ako-chan leans back in her seat. Every friend is close beside her now. The long road brought them all together. One by one, they came back to her. Now they fly on through the sky. Ako-chan is glad down to her heart. The family is whole again at last.
