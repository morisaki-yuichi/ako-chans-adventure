The flying boat comes down towards the coast. White rocks shine below in the bright sun. The Adriatic Sea is wide and blue. Ako-chan knows this place at once. It is the Dalmatian coast in Croatia. Her heart beats fast and glad.

The boat lands soft on the pale stone. Ako-chan steps out onto the sunny rocks. The sea air smells of salt and heat. Then she sees it. A small round shape runs across the stone. It runs fast, straight towards her feet. It is Dalu. He is bigger now. But he still fits in her two hands. "Ako-chan! You came back!" he says. He spins a quick, happy circle. His little legs carry him at top speed.

Ako-chan looks up at the tall white cliffs. She met Dalu here three years ago. He was a tiny baby then, no bigger than her hand. He came out from a crack in these rocks. Now he is back, on the same bright shore. The place looks just the same in the sun.

Dango barks loud and glad beside her. His long tail wags fast and wide. He knows this little tortoise well. Dalu runs to Dango, then back to her. Obia stands near the boat and watches it all. The shore is not quiet now. The blue sea shines behind them all. A warm wind comes up off the water.

Ako-chan kneels down on the smooth stone. She holds Dalu close to her chest. He is warm and round and real. "Hello, Dalu," she says softly. "I missed you." Dalu looks up with bright, quick eyes. He is still the same brave little friend. The first friend is back with them again.
