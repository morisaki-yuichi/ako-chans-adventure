The flying boat comes down over the sand. Red rock walls rise high all around. Red sand spreads wide below the sun. Ako-chan knows this hot place well. It is Wadi Rum, the desert in Jordan. The heat comes up to meet the boat.

Ako-chan puts on her light silver cloth. It is thin and cool against the heat. It sends the hot air away from her. She steps down onto the warm sand. The dry air is very hot here. Then she looks up at the high rocks. A small tortoise sits on a tall stone. It does not move. It only watches. It is Gabu. He is bigger now, but still small. He sits calm and straight in the sun. He waits, patient as ever.

Ako-chan met Gabu here three years ago. Back then, he watched her from far away. He took many days to trust her then. He came to her slow, step by step. Gabu is careful. He was always careful. Some things never change at all.

Dalu rolls forward fast in his red Pod. "Gabu! Gabu! We came back!" he says. But Gabu does not rush down to them. He looks at each face, slow and still. The heat shimmers in the air between them. Dango pants hard beside Ako-chan's leg. The sand lifts in the dry, warm wind. Obia stands still and watches the high rock.

Gabu looks at Ako-chan for a long moment. Then, slow and sure, he begins to climb down. He does not hurry. He never does. Three years are gone now. But Gabu is still Gabu. Quiet, calm, and full of patience.
