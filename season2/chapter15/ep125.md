The flying boat turns towards the south. It flies on high above the sea. Below, the ocean spreads wide and grey. The land is far behind them now. Ako-chan looks out the long window. The whole world is sky and sea.

Ako-chan thinks back three years now. Back then, they sailed south on a ship. They crossed the open ocean on the waves. The water rocked them slow, day and night. The sea was their road for many days. Now they fly high above that same place. The ship is gone. This time, they have wings.

The engine hums, deep and low. Dango sleeps close at her feet. Suke-san watches the sky, sharp and still. Daru and Gabu rest in their Pods. Ruka is quiet in her yellow Pod. Obia stands steady by the controls. No one speaks for a long while. The deep hum of the boat fills the air. Each friend is close. Each one is safe.

Clouds drift past, white and thin. The air grows cold outside the boat. The dark sea moves far below. The sun sinks low across the waves. The light turns gold, then grey, then dim. The cold creeps in along the cabin floor. Ako-chan pulls her coat in close. Far ahead, the sky is pale and clear. Something waits at the end of the world.

The boat flies on through the quiet sky. The water below grows cold and white. Ako-chan looks far across the sea. A pale shape lies at the world's far edge. A great white land, far away. It is still far off. But it draws near. Their long road leads them there.
