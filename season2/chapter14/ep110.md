The next day is calm and gray. The small Pod sits on the grass. Ruka stands beside it now. She looks at it for a long moment. Ako-chan waits close by. She does not say a word. She wants Ruka to choose for herself.

At last, Ruka steps into the Pod. It is soft and warm inside. She takes one slow step. The Pod rolls a little way. Then she stops. The Pod stops too. Ruka waits and looks around. Then she walks again. The yellow light glows as she moves. Stop. Look. Walk. That is how Ruka goes.

Ako-chan sits on the grass and watches. She does not push the Pod. She does not call out. Some animals would rush ahead. Ruka never does. She moves at her own slow pace. Time seems to slow down with her. Dango lies nearby with his head down. He watches the yellow Pod roll past. The garden is quiet all around them.

The Pod turns in a slow, wide circle. Ruka stops to look at a leaf. Then she walks on again. Slowly, she learns the Pod. She knows when to walk now. She knows when to rest. The Pod feels more like home each hour.

Ruka rolls a short way more. Then she rests inside the warm Pod. Ako-chan smiles at her. "Take your time," she says softly. Ruka pulls her head out and looks back.

Ruka learns the Pod her own way. Slow. Careful. One step at a time. She will not go fast. She does not need to. Ruka's way is Ruka's way. And that is enough for now.
