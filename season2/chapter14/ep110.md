The next day is calm and grey. The small Pod sits on the grass. Luca stands beside it now. She looks at it for a long moment. Ako-chan waits close by. She does not say a word. She wants Luca to choose for herself.

At last, Luca steps into the Pod. It is soft and warm inside. She takes one slow step. The Pod rolls a little way. Then she stops. The Pod stops too. Luca waits and looks around. Then she walks again. The yellow light glows as she moves. Stop. Look. Walk. That is how Luca goes.

Ako-chan sits on the grass and watches. She does not push the Pod. She does not call out. Some animals would rush ahead. Luca never does. She moves at her own slow pace. Time seems to slow down with her. Dango lies nearby with his head down. He watches the yellow Pod roll past. The garden is quiet all around them.

The Pod turns in a slow, wide circle. Luca stops to look at a leaf. Then she walks on again. Slowly, she learns the Pod. She knows when to walk now. She knows when to rest. The Pod feels more like home each hour.

Luca rolls a short way more. Then she rests inside the warm Pod. Ako-chan smiles at her. "Take your time," she says softly. Luca pulls her head out and looks back.

Luca learns the Pod her own way. Slow. Careful. One step at a time. She will not go fast. She does not need to. Luca's way is Luca's way. And that is enough for now.
