The garden is bright this afternoon. Ako-chan and Obia work on the grass. Small parts lie all around them. There are screws, wires, and bits of metal. A round shape sits between them. It is new. It is very small.

For days, the two of them built it together. Obia's paws move fast and sure. Ako-chan holds each part in place. The garden fills with small clicks and taps. Now Obia fits the last piece in. The little Pod is ready at last. It is smaller than the ones Obia made before. A soft yellow light glows inside it. The light is gentle and warm.

Obia turns to the windowsill. Luca rests there in the sun. "This one is for you, Luca," Obia says. Its voice is flat and kind. Ako-chan lifts Luca gently in her hands. She carries her to the grass. She sets her down beside the Pod.

Luca does not move at first. She looks at the round yellow shape. The light glows low, then a little brighter. Ako-chan kneels close beside her. The wind moves through the trees. A bird calls far away. Dango watches from the doorway. He does not come near the new thing.

Luca takes one slow step towards it. Then she stops. She pulls her head in. The Pod waits, quiet and still. Ako-chan does not rush her at all. She just waits beside her.

The new Pod sits in the grass. Its yellow glow is warm and low. Luca looks at it for a long time. She does not climb in. She circles it once, slow and unsure. But her eyes stay on the small round thing. Something new is waiting for her.
