The morning is clear and calm. The flying boat stands in the garden. It is finished now, at last. The early sun touches its wings. They shine soft and gold.

Ako-chan steps back to look at it. The work is over. The boat is ready. Its great wings spread wide and high. Its body sits low on the grass. Days of work made this. Ako-chan can hardly believe it. She stands very still and looks up.

Dango sits at her feet and looks up too. His ears are high. His tail is still. Ruka rests in her Pod nearby. She lifts her small head towards the wings. Obia stands beside Ako-chan, quiet. Its pale blue eyes shine in the light. All of them look up at the boat. They all feel the same quiet hope.

The wings shine like still water. The morning light runs along the metal. Every part is in its place now. The boat looks strong and ready to fly. It is bigger than their old ship was. Ako-chan feels proud and a little afraid. So much waits beyond the fence.

The garden is calm this morning. No tool moves. No one speaks. The light grows brighter on the wings. A soft wind moves across the grass. The big boat waits, ready and still. Ako-chan holds her breath and waits.

Ako-chan rests a hand on the cool hull. Three years ago, they walked away. Now they will rise into the sky. She looks at her small family here. Dango. Ruka. Obia. And her. Her heart beats hard and fast. This is the day they fly. Only the sky is left now.
