The morning sky is wide and blue. Everyone is aboard now. Ako-chan sits at the front. Dango lies close at her feet. Ruka is safe in her mini Pod. Obia stands beside the controls.

Obia starts the engine. It hums, then roars to life. The whole boat shakes and shudders. Ako-chan holds the seat tight. "Hold on," she says to them all. Then, slowly, the boat lifts up. The grass falls away below them. They rise above the garden. They rise above the house.

The lift feels strange and light. Ako-chan's stomach drops a little. The ground pulls at her, then lets go. She grips the seat with both hands. Her heart beats fast and high. They are really flying now. The whole world tilts below her. The garden is far below already.

Ako-chan looks out the window. England spreads out far below. The green fields grow small and neat. The roads turn into thin gray lines. Her whole home looks tiny now. The sea shines far past the coast. Clouds drift between her and the ground. She never saw the world like this before.

The boat climbs higher into the clouds. White mist wraps around the wings. The engine hums, deep and steady. Ako-chan feels it in her chest. She never felt so high before. Dango lifts his ears but stays close. Ruka stays calm inside her Pod. The cold blue sky opens wide ahead.

Ako-chan turns the boat toward the south. The signal calls them on, far away. Three years ago, they left on foot. Now they fly above the world. The long road begins again. This time, it begins in the sky.
