The quiet days go on, much the same. The park is part of every afternoon now. Ako-chan brings the friends along the same road. The road there is short and well known. They take the same bench by the trees. The animals know this place well. Nothing here surprises them anymore. The walk is an old and gentle habit.

Each day is much like the one before. Day after day, it is the same gentle round. The sky changes colour, from pale to gold. More leaves fall and gather on the paths. Dango sniffs the same trees every time. The three Pods roll to their usual spots. The same dog-walkers pass at their usual hour. Suke-san watches the park, calm and still. Ako-chan sits and listens to the soft voices. Nothing special happens here. And that is good.

The wooden bench is smooth and cool beneath her. A cold wind moves through the bare branches. Birds call softly from the high trees. The grass is stiff with frost in the morning. By afternoon it softens, damp and green. A squirrel runs along a low branch. The afternoon light grows long and low. Ako-chan watches the people far away. They come and go across the wide green park. It is an ordinary, peaceful day.

Ako-chan leans back and closes her eyes. The friends rest quietly all around her. It is just a walk, like any other. Then she opens her eyes and looks up. Far across the park, someone stands alone. A still figure, watching from the trees. Ako-chan does not know him yet. But there he is, quiet in the distance.
